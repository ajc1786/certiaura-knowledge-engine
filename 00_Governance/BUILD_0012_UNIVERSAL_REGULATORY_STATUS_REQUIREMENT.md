# UNIVERSAL REGULATORY & STATUS INTELLIGENCE REQUIREMENT

**Decision date:** 2026-07-15  
**Status:** LOCKED PLATFORM REQUIREMENT

## Decision
The regulatory/status model is mandatory for **all CertiAura peptides and compounds**.

## Rules
1. Regulatory claims must be dated.
2. Status must be jurisdiction-specific.
3. Approval, prescribing, compounding, sale, possession, online availability and anti-doping status are separate concepts.
4. Material change triggers source capture, status update, claim review and commercial-boundary review.
5. Stale records must be visibly flagged.
6. Historical builds are not silently rewritten.
7. Public-facing current-status claims must trace to a dated source record.
