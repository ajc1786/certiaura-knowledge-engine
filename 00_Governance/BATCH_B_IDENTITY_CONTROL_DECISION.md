# BATCH B IDENTITY CONTROL DECISION

**Date:** 2026-07-16

## Locked controls

1. `GHK-Cu` means the copper complex of Gly-His-Lys unless a source specifies otherwise.
2. Full-length `thymosin beta-4` is distinct from the marketed `TB-500` fragment.
3. `Melanotan II` is distinct from afamelanotide / Melanotan I.
4. `Epitalon` is distinct from the bovine pineal extract epithalamin.
5. Evidence may not be transferred between these identities without explicit source support.
