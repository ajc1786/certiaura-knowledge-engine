# CERTIAURA BATCH PRODUCTION STANDARD

**Version:** 1.0.0  
**Effective date:** 2026-07-16

## Objective

Increase production velocity without weakening:

- permanent identifiers;
- source integrity;
- evidence grading;
- safety controls;
- regulatory monitoring;
- one-source-of-truth architecture.

## Standard batch structure

### Stage 1 — Batch Bootstrap

For every item:

1. Clean and deduplicate name.
2. Classify peptide, compound, blend or ancillary product.
3. Confirm aliases and identity ambiguity.
4. Allocate permanent UAI only after identity control.
5. Create mechanism and relationship foundation.
6. Create evidence leads.
7. Create safety and monitoring foundation.
8. Create regulatory status record.
9. Create claim controls and commercial boundary.
10. Record certification blockers.

### Stage 2 — Batch Evidence Expansion

Across the whole batch:

- capture the highest-value primary sources;
- update source closure;
- extract study design and outcomes;
- strengthen safety translation;
- update evidence maturity.

## Release rule

Batching changes production quantity, not evidence standards.

No asset may inherit another asset's evidence or regulatory status merely because both appear in the same batch.
