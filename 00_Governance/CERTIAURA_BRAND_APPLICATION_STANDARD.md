# CERTIAURA BRAND APPLICATION STANDARD

**Version:** 1.0.0  
**Effective date:** 2026-07-15  
**Status:** LOCKED DEFAULT

The approved CertiAura identity must appear on Project Genesis and future branded outputs, including knowledge assets, reports, printable PDFs, Academy materials, Product Passport™ outputs, Cost Intelligence™ reports and controlled marketplace documents.

## Canonical identity
**Brand:** CertiAura  
**Tagline:** EVIDENCE. CLARITY. CONFIDENCE.

## Rules
1. Do not stretch or distort the logo.
2. Use the light UI asset on pale backgrounds and the dark banner for premium dark covers.
3. Use the app icon only for application chrome, icons and favicons.
4. Do not recolour outside approved variants.
5. Branding does not alter scientific, regulatory or certification status.
6. Future templates inherit the branded header by default.
7. Unbranded output requires an explicit reason.
8. The artwork's registered-mark treatment does not itself prove legal registration status.
