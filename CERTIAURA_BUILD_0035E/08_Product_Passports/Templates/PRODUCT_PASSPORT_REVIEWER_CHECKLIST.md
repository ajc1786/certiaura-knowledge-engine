## A. Source control

- [ ] Installed Build 0035D validator passed.
- [ ] Reviewed submission snapshot has been preserved.
- [ ] SHA-256 of reviewed snapshot recorded.
- [ ] Submission identifier and schema version confirmed.
- [ ] Duplicate or superseding submission check completed.

## B. Identity and declarations

- [ ] Supplier legal identity is traceable.
- [ ] Supplier authority to submit is recorded.
- [ ] Manufacturer identity is assessed where different.
- [ ] Product identifier and label name align.
- [ ] Batch number is legible and consistent across applicable evidence.
- [ ] Supplier declarations are complete.

## C. Evidence integrity and provenance

For every evidence object:

- [ ] Hash present and file available.
- [ ] Alterations and redactions assessed.
- [ ] Source channel recorded.
- [ ] Issuer traced or directly confirmed where required.
- [ ] Report number and dates checked.
- [ ] Accreditation claim checked against scope where relevant.
- [ ] Product applicability confirmed.
- [ ] Batch applicability confirmed where required.
- [ ] Method and result scope supports the linked claim.
- [ ] Expiry or validity assessed.
- [ ] Conflicts with other evidence recorded.

## D. Claim decisions

For every claim:

- [ ] Evidence links resolve.
- [ ] Claim wording does not exceed evidence scope.
- [ ] Evidence class awarded by reviewer, not copied from supplier.
- [ ] Batch-specific claim has confirmed batch linkage.
- [ ] Verification decision recorded.
- [ ] Scope statement and limitations recorded.
- [ ] Public-display approval explicitly decided.
- [ ] Next review date recorded where time-sensitive.

## E. Risk and escalation

- [ ] All nine risk domains graded.
- [ ] Overall risk is not lower than unresolved material risk.
- [ ] Critical flags recorded.
- [ ] Integrity, identity, batch mismatch and misrepresentation triggers assessed.
- [ ] Clarification, quarantine or rejection used where required.

## F. Final decision

### For VERIFIED only

- [ ] Intake controls all passed.
- [ ] Every publicly displayed claim is VERIFIED.
- [ ] Every VERIFIED claim has E4 or E5 evidence.
- [ ] No rejected, quarantined or expired evidence supports a VERIFIED claim.
- [ ] No open critical flags.
- [ ] Overall risk is LOW or MEDIUM.
- [ ] Lead reviewer and final approver are different people.
- [ ] Final approver conflict declared and absent.
- [ ] Effective-until date recorded.
- [ ] Record marked immutable.

### Marketplace

- [ ] Marketplace eligibility separately assessed.
- [ ] VERIFIED status has not been treated as automatic legal permission to list or sell.
- [ ] Legal or regulatory route is not represented as resolved unless separately evidenced.
