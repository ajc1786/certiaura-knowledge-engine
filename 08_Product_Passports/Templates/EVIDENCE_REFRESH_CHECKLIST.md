# Evidence Refresh Checklist

- [ ] New submission identifier allocated.
- [ ] Every evidence item has a permanent identifier.
- [ ] SHA-256 hash recorded for every document.
- [ ] Source and provenance confirmed.
- [ ] Batch-specific evidence matches the affected batch.
- [ ] Issue, receipt and review dates recorded.
- [ ] Superseded evidence is referenced, not overwritten.
- [ ] Mandatory critical evidence is present.
- [ ] Independent reviewer assigned.
- [ ] Review outcome recorded for every item.
- [ ] Rejected or quarantined evidence is excluded from positive decisions.
- [ ] Claim-to-evidence links updated.
- [ ] Effective and next-review dates proposed.
