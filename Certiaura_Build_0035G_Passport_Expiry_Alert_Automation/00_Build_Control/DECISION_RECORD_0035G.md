# DECISION RECORD — CERT-BUILD-0035G

**Decision:** Add Product Passport™ expiry, trigger monitoring and alert automation.  
**Status:** Founder authorised — GREEN.  
**Date:** 2026-07-18.

## Existing decision extended

Build 0035F established time-bound publication, lifecycle triggers, suspension, expiry and separate marketplace eligibility.

## Reason

Those controls require repeatable operational detection. Manual-only review creates a risk of silent expiry, delayed suspension and stale marketplace representation.

## Decision implemented

Add a read-only monitoring engine, alert model, routing controls, threshold matrices and semantic validation. Automatic outputs are restricted to protective or escalatory actions. Automatic verification, publication, reinstatement and marketplace approval are prohibited.

## Effect on previous builds

Builds 0035D, 0035E and 0035F are retained unchanged. Build 0035G consumes their records and rules.
