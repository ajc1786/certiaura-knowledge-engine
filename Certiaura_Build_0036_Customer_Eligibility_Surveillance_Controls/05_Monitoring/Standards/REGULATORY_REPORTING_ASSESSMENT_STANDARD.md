# Regulatory Reporting Assessment Standard

This standard records whether a competent, authorised reviewer determines that an external report is required for a specific jurisdiction and event. The build does not determine applicable law and does not contain a final legal route for peptide sales.

Mandatory fields include jurisdiction, authority or reporting route, assessment basis, reportability decision, decision maker, independent reviewer for serious/critical cases, decision date, deadline, submission reference where applicable and follow-up status.

Automation may calculate deadlines and warn of overdue actions. It may not decide reportability or submit reports.
