## Critical catalogue controls

- `HCG` does not define urinary versus recombinant product identity.
- `HMG` is a variable gonadotropin mixture and must not be treated as one chemically uniform peptide.
- `Insulin, 3 mL` does not define insulin type, concentration, formulation or device.
- `EPO` does not define epoetin alfa, epoetin beta, darbepoetin or another erythropoiesis-stimulating agent.
- Oxytocin, teriparatide, insulin, HCG/HMG and erythropoiesis-stimulating agents have authorised medicinal contexts, but catalogue products do not inherit those authorisations.
- HCG is not an approved weight-loss medicine.
- Insulin and erythropoietin carry high-consequence dosing and anti-doping risks.
- No personal dosing, fertility protocol, labour-induction protocol, insulin plan or performance-enhancement protocol is created.
