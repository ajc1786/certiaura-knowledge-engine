# Communication Channel and Approval Matrix

| Channel | Commercial presumption | AI personalisation | Minimum approval |
|---|---:|---:|---|
| Internal research record | No | No | Scientific owner |
| Knowledge asset | Context-dependent | Limited | Scientific reviewer |
| Professional report | Context-dependent | Controlled | Scientific; medical/legal when high risk |
| Academy material | Usually no | Limited | Scientific + communications |
| Product Passport™ | Product-linked | No autonomous recommendation | Product Passport reviewer + scientific |
| Marketplace | Yes | Ranking may be material | Scientific + commercial + communications |
| Affiliate page | Yes | Ranking may be material | Scientific + commercial + communications; disclosure mandatory |
| Website public page | Context-dependent | High risk | Scientific + communications; legal/regulatory when applicable |
| AI chat | Context-dependent | Yes | Automated gates; human review for high risk |
| Email campaign | Yes | Possible | Communications + commercial; legal/regulatory when applicable |
| Social media | Yes when brand/product linked | No high-risk personalisation | Communications + scientific |
| Paid advertisement | Yes | No autonomous health decision | Legal/regulatory + communications + scientific |

## Non-transfer rule

Approval for one channel does not automatically authorise another. Public, paid, marketplace, affiliate and social channels require explicit scope in the approval record.
