# Tesamorelin Source Quality Scoring Standard

Build: `0058`

Each governed source must be scored separately for provenance, methodological quality, recency, direct relevance, limitations and evidence position. Aggregate scoring must not conceal rejected provenance, out-of-scope evidence or critical quality gaps. Human review is mandatory before an evidence set can advance.

This standard governs non-clinical knowledge architecture only. It does not authorise diagnosis, dosing, prescribing, treatment selection or clinical recommendation.
