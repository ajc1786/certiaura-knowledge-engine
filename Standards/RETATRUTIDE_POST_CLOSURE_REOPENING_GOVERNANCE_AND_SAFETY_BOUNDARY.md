# Retatrutide Post-Closure Reopening Governance and Safety Boundary

**Build provenance:** CERT-BUILD-0051

- Direct identifiers are prohibited.
- Artificial intelligence cannot approve reopening, close urgent routing or issue treatment instructions.
- A closed record is immutable and may only be superseded by a linked governed state transition.
- Urgent-routing state cannot be downgraded by routine surveillance outcomes.
- Recurrence analytics are descriptive and require human review.
- Missing or conflicting evidence produces `INSUFFICIENT_DATA` or `MORE_INFORMATION_REQUIRED`.
