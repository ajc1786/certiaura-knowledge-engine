# OV05 — Identity / Composition Quarantine Overlay

## Requirements

1. Submission cannot proceed beyond identity review
2. No mechanism, efficacy, safety, comparator or commercial evaluation
3. Direct manufacturer declaration and independent orthogonal identity required
4. Human review required before quarantine release
5. No automatic release from a supplier certificate of analysis

## Control

This overlay supplements the primary submission class and cannot reduce its requirements.
