# OV03 — Investigational or Trial-Material Comparator Overlay

## Requirements

1. Sponsor, trial identifier, phase, status and exact study material
2. Sequence/formulation/manufacturing information available in public sources
3. Explicit statement that trial evidence does not validate supplier material
4. No claim that recruitment, completion or publication equals approval
5. Living-evidence review after material registry or publication change

## Control

This overlay supplements the primary submission class and cannot reduce its requirements.
