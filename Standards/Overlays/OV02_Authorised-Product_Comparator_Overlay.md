# OV02 — Authorised-Product Comparator Overlay

## Requirements

1. Exact authorised product, jurisdiction, manufacturer and current label
2. Active-substance and finished-product comparability strategy
3. Side-by-side formulation, strength, route and container comparison
4. No approval, indication or safety-label transfer without demonstrated equivalence
5. Current regulatory verification and material-change trigger

## Control

This overlay supplements the primary submission class and cannot reduce its requirements.
