# OV01 — Parenteral-Risk or Sterile-Claim Overlay

## Requirements

1. Exact sterile or non-sterile claim and intended laboratory/product presentation
2. Contamination control strategy or equivalent manufacturing-risk summary
3. Aseptic or terminal-sterilisation process validation
4. Batch sterility test and bacterial-endotoxin result
5. Visible and subvisible particulate control
6. Container-closure integrity
7. Environmental monitoring and media-fill status where applicable
8. Lyophilisation process validation where applicable
9. No administration or reconstitution claim unless separately lawful and authorised

## Control

This overlay supplements the primary submission class and cannot reduce its requirements.
