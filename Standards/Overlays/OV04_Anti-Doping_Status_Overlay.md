# OV04 — Anti-Doping Status Overlay

## Requirements

1. Current WADA Prohibited List review date
2. Exact category, in-competition/out-of-competition scope and athlete population where relevant
3. No athlete-permitted claim from absence of an exact name
4. Annual review and immediate update after a material list or interpretation change

## Control

This overlay supplements the primary submission class and cannot reduce its requirements.
