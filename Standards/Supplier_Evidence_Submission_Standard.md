# SUPPLIER EVIDENCE REQUEST & PRODUCT PASSPORT SUBMISSION STANDARD

**Build:** 0035D  
**Version:** 1.0.0  
**Effective date:** 2026-07-17  
**Estate covered:** 102 product-family assets

## Purpose

This standard defines the evidence CertiAura requires before supplier assertions may be incorporated into a Product Passport™, scientific comparison, marketplace record or commercial-release review.

It is an **evidence intake and review standard**. It is not:

- a manufacturing authorisation;
- Good Manufacturing Practice certification;
- product approval;
- supplier approval;
- a safety or efficacy determination;
- permission for human administration;
- automatic commercial release.

## Governing principles

1. **Manufacturer, not catalogue, is the evidence source.**
2. **Batch-specific evidence takes precedence over generic marketing documents.**
3. **Identity requires orthogonal confirmation.**
4. **Area-percent purity is not peptide content, potency or sterile quality.**
5. **A certificate of analysis is not accepted without supplier qualification and test-result verification.**
6. **Authorised and trial products are non-equivalent until directly demonstrated otherwise.**
7. **Sterility, endotoxin and particulate evidence are separate quality attributes.**
8. **Electronic and paper records must be attributable, legible, contemporaneous, original, accurate, complete, consistent, enduring and available.**
9. **Critical identity or composition conflicts remain quarantined.**
10. **No supplier submission automatically changes canonical facts or opens a direct-sale gate.**

## Submission decision states

| State | Meaning |
|---|---|
| RECEIVED | Files received but not screened |
| ADMINISTRATIVELY COMPLETE | Required file classes are present |
| TECHNICAL REVIEW | Scientific and quality review in progress |
| DEFICIENT | Material evidence absent, inconsistent or unsuitable |
| QUARANTINED | Identity/composition prevents further progression |
| ACCEPTED FOR PRODUCT PASSPORT REVIEW | Evidence may populate reviewed passport fields |
| REJECTED | Fraud, material inconsistency, unresolvable provenance or unacceptable risk |
| SUPERSEDED | Replaced by a later approved submission version |

## Non-automatic controls

Even an accepted submission does not establish:

- lawful human use;
- product approval;
- clinical efficacy or safety;
- authorised-product equivalence;
- commercial release;
- Platinum certification.

Human review and recorded approval are mandatory.
