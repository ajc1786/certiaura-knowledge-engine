# BPC-157 Evidence Reconstruction Standard

## Purpose

Reconstruct a target-specific, source-verifiable BPC-157 evidence pack without transferring Tesamorelin biological, safety, efficacy, monitoring or clinical assumptions.

## Mandatory evidence tiers

1. Government regulator or official compounding material.
2. Sport-integrity authority where athlete applicability exists.
3. Human trial registry.
4. Peer-reviewed evidence synthesis with explicit human-evidence limitations.
5. Product-quality and manufacturing uncertainty evidence.

## Controlled baseline as at 23 July 2026

- FDA's July 2026 briefing identifies quality, impurity, aggregation and immunogenicity concerns and proposes that BPC-157 free base and acetate not be included on the 503A Bulks List. This is recorded as a regulatory proposal, not a final determination.
- The 2026 WADA Prohibited List names BPC-157 under S0 and prohibits it at all times for covered athletes.
- ClinicalTrials.gov lists a recruiting Phase 2 hamstring-strain trial with no posted results.
- The indexed review describes broad preclinical literature but very limited human evidence and treats the substance as investigational.

## Decision rule

Architecture fit cannot convert missing human evidence, unresolved safety or quality concerns, sport prohibition or regulatory uncertainty into an approval. The default transition outcome is HOLD or CONDITIONAL_HOLD until the target-specific board accepts a version-locked evidence pack.

This standard is non-clinical and provides no dosing, prescribing, diagnosis or treatment recommendation.
