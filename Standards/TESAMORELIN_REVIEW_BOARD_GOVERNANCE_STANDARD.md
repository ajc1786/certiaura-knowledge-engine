# Tesamorelin Review Board Governance Standard

Build: `0059`

The governed Tesamorelin review board requires an identified chair, evidence reviewer, safety reviewer and governance reviewer. Every voting member must disclose conflicts and confirm independence. Approval requires quorum, a version-locked evidence pack, explicit rationale and named approvers. Missing roles, undisclosed conflicts or unresolved critical conditions result in a hold or rejection.

This is a non-clinical knowledge-governance standard. It does not authorise diagnosis, dosing, prescribing, treatment selection or clinical recommendation.
