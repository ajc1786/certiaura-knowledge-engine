# Retatrutide Reusable Architecture Handoff Standard

**Build:** 0055

Reusable controls are limited to generic repository, governance, validation, transactional, audit and closure mechanisms. Retatrutide-specific evidence, claims, thresholds, schemas, monitoring parameters and safety conclusions are excluded by default.

A handoff may be `APPROVED_FOR_GOVERNED_PILOT`, `PILOT_ONLY`, `HOLD` or `REJECT`. Every target peptide must receive its own evidence corpus, biological model, safety boundaries, monitoring logic, schemas, examples, validators and human review.
