# Tesamorelin Target-Specific Data Contract Standard

Tesamorelin records must use target-specific schemas and identifiers. Generic architecture may be reused, but Retatrutide claims, thresholds, safety conclusions, monitoring parameters and acceptance states must not be copied as scientific truth.

Every contract must:

- identify Build 0056 and Tesamorelin;
- prohibit clinical equivalence and autonomous clinical decision support;
- include human approval where a decision advances the pilot;
- fail closed when required evidence, boundaries, escalation rules or stop conditions are absent;
- preserve evidence gaps and uncertainty explicitly.
