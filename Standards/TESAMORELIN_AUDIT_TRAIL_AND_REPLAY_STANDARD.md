# Tesamorelin Audit Trail and Replay Standard

Build: `0057`

Every simulated ingestion, review, monitoring, hold, escalation and acceptance event must retain deterministic identifiers, provenance links, timestamps, actor identity, input state, decision state and output state. Replay must reproduce the recorded route without changing source evidence or converting a controlled hold into an acceptance.

Audit replay is an architecture assurance control only and is not clinical decision support.
