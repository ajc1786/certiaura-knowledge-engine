# Tesamorelin Evidence Ingestion and Provenance Standard

Build: `0057`

Every source entering the governed Tesamorelin pilot must have a stable source identifier, source class, locator, retrieval timestamp, SHA-256 digest, evidence scope, provenance state and named human reviewer.

Accepted ingestion is limited to non-clinical knowledge-workflow simulation. It does not authorise diagnosis, prescribing, dosing, treatment selection or clinical recommendation. Duplicate source identities, missing hashes, unverifiable provenance and unsupported clinical claims are fail-closed conditions.
