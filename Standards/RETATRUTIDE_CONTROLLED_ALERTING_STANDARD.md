# Retatrutide Controlled Alerting Standard

## Requirements

1. Alerts are policy evaluations, not diagnoses.
2. Urgent routing locks take precedence over all other states.
3. Administrative review state takes precedence over descriptive metric thresholds.
4. Threshold alerts require sufficient observations and configured evidence references.
5. Direct identifiers must be rejected.
6. Alerts must never recommend dosing, titration, treatment selection or procurement.
