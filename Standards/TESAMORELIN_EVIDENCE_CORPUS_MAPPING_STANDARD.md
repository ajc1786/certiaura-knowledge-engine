# Tesamorelin Evidence Corpus Mapping Standard

## Purpose

This standard governs the first target-specific knowledge-architecture pilot selected by Build 0055. It defines how Tesamorelin evidence is identified, cited, classified, reviewed and bounded before any implementation decision.

## Mandatory controls

1. Evidence sources must be individually identifiable and traceable.
2. Regulatory, peer-reviewed, guidance and safety sources must remain distinguishable.
3. Absence of evidence must be recorded as a gap, not converted into a positive claim.
4. Target-specific evidence must not be inferred from Retatrutide or another peptide.
5. Backfilled or secondary evidence must be labelled accurately.
6. Every accepted map requires human scientific and evidence-governance review.

## Boundary

This is a knowledge-governance standard. It does not authorise prescribing, dosing, diagnosis, treatment or individual clinical decision support.
