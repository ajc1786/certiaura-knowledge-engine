# Scientific Claim Classification and Decision Matrix

| Trigger | Class | Minimum evidence | Review | Default outcome if missing |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Identity/composition fact | FACTUAL_IDENTITY | Authoritative product or reference record | Content owner | Hold |
| Mechanism or study summary | SCIENTIFIC_EDUCATIONAL | Direct evidence object and limitations | Scientific | Qualify/Hold |
| Outcome or health benefit | HEALTH | Robust evidence proportionate to wording | Scientific + communications | Reject/Hold |
| Prevents, treats, diagnoses or cures | MEDICINAL | Lawful product route and substantiation | Medical + legal/regulatory | Block |
| Safety, no-risk or no-side-effect wording | SAFETY | Comprehensive evidence; absolute wording normally prohibited | Medical + legal/regulatory | Block |
| Comparative superiority | COMPARATIVE | Like-for-like current evidence and method | Scientific + communications | Reject/Hold |
| Price, supplier or product ranking | COMMERCIAL | Current data, method and disclosure | Commercial + communications | Hold |
| Personal story or endorsement | TESTIMONIAL | Authenticity plus claim controls | Communications | Reject/Hold |
| AI selects an option for a person | AI_RECOMMENDATION | Approved evidence, human review and applicable regulated/data controls | High-risk route | Block |

## Wording calibration

- **Established:** supported by strong, direct and consistent evidence.
- **Supported:** evidence is material but limitations remain.
- **Preliminary:** early or indirect evidence; no outcome certainty.
- **Hypothesis-generating:** mechanistic, preclinical or exploratory only.
- **Not established:** evidence is absent, insufficient or not product-specific.

The wording used publicly must never exceed the recorded evidence calibration.
