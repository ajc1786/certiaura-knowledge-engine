# CHANGE CONTROL & RESUBMISSION STANDARD

A new submission or formal change notification is mandatory after any change to:

- manufacturer or manufacturing site;
- synthesis, expression, extraction or purification process;
- starting materials, cell bank, tissue source or supplier;
- sequence, terminal chemistry, salt, conjugation or composition;
- analytical method or specification;
- formulation, excipient, container closure or device;
- sterilisation, aseptic processing or lyophilisation process;
- storage, transport, expiry or retest period;
- authorised, trial or regulatory status;
- complaint, recall, warning or material safety signal.

## Change classification

| Class | Meaning | Action |
|---|---|---|
| C1 | Editorial/no quality impact | Record and review |
| C2 | Minor controlled quality change | Targeted evidence update |
| C3 | Major identity, process, formulation or site change | Full technical reassessment |
| C4 | Critical safety, authenticity or regulatory change | Immediate quarantine |

No change is accepted silently.
