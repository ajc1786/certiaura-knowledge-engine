# STERILE-QUALITY EVIDENCE STANDARD

Sterile-product evidence is required separately from chemical identity and purity.

## Required package

- manufacturing-site and cleanroom classification;
- contamination control strategy;
- sterilisation or aseptic-process description;
- process validation and media-fill status;
- environmental and personnel monitoring summary;
- sterile filtration validation where used;
- lyophilisation aseptic controls where used;
- batch sterility test;
- bacterial-endotoxin test;
- visible and subvisible particulate results;
- bioburden before sterilising filtration where applicable;
- container-closure integrity;
- hold-time and transport controls;
- deviation, investigation and batch-disposition summary.

## Prohibited shortcuts

- “sterile” stated only on a label;
- sterility inferred from low bioburden;
- endotoxin inferred from sterility;
- reliance on a distributor certificate without manufacturer evidence;
- universal compatibility or reconstitution claim.
