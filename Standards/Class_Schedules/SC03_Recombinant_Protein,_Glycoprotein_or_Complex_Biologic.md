# SC03 — Recombinant Protein, Glycoprotein or Complex Biologic

## Scope

Recombinant protein, glycoprotein, hormone, complex biological active or product whose identity depends on expression system, higher-order structure, glycosylation, potency or manufacturing process.

## Mandatory evidence

1. Molecular/proper name and complete primary sequence or subunit composition
2. Expression system, host cell, construct and cell-bank provenance
3. Manufacturer, manufacturing site and biological licence or GMP status
4. Upstream and downstream process summary
5. Glycosylation, post-translational modification and charge-variant profile
6. Intact mass, peptide mapping and orthogonal identity
7. Biological potency assay with qualified reference standard
8. Aggregation, fragments, particles and higher-order-structure controls
9. Host-cell proteins, residual host-cell DNA and process impurities
10. Viral/adventitious-agent and transmissible-agent controls
11. Formulation, container closure, cold-chain and stability evidence
12. Sterility, endotoxin and batch-release evidence for sterile presentation

## Minimum analytical methods

- Peptide mapping and intact mass
- Potency bioassay
- SEC or equivalent aggregation method
- Process-impurity and host-cell testing

## Automatic rejection or quarantine triggers

- Expression system or manufacturer omitted
- Potency reported only as mass without bioassay
- No glycosylation or aggregation control where applicable

## Release rule

Completion of this schedule permits Product Passport technical review only. It does not establish approval, safety, efficacy, human-use suitability, direct sale or Platinum status.
