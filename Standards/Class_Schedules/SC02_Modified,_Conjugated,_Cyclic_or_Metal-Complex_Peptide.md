# SC02 — Modified, Conjugated, Cyclic or Metal-Complex Peptide

## Scope

Peptide containing lipidation, PEGylation, acetylation, amidation, non-natural residues, D-retro-inverso design, cyclisation, metal complexation or another identity-defining modification.

## Mandatory evidence

1. All SC01 requirements
2. Exact modification, linker or conjugating-group chemical structure
3. Modification or attachment site and site-occupancy result
4. Free/unmodified peptide and over-modified species profile
5. Conjugate, lipid, PEG or metal stoichiometry and distribution
6. Cyclisation or bridge identity where applicable
7. Stereochemical impurity and epimer assessment
8. Orthogonal characterisation of modified and unmodified species
9. Modification-specific stability and degradation pathways
10. Potency or receptor-binding comparability where scientifically justified

## Minimum analytical methods

- LC-MS intact mass and peptide mapping
- HPLC/UPLC purity and assay
- Modification-specific orthogonal method
- Free-peptide or unconjugated-species assay

## Automatic rejection or quarantine triggers

- Modification described only by trade name
- No attachment-site or occupancy evidence
- Copper, PEG, lipid or bridge chemistry omitted

## Release rule

Completion of this schedule permits Product Passport technical review only. It does not establish approval, safety, efficacy, human-use suitability, direct sale or Platinum status.
