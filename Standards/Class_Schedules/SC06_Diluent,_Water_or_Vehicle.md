# SC06 — Diluent, Water or Vehicle

## Scope

Water, preserved water, acidified water or another vehicle presented for laboratory, pharmaceutical or parenteral-risk use.

## Mandatory evidence

1. Exact composition and concentration of every ingredient
2. Water grade and manufacturing-source qualification
3. pH, osmolality, conductivity and appearance specifications
4. Preservative identity and assay where applicable
5. Sterility and bacterial-endotoxin evidence for sterile claims
6. Bioburden and objectionable-organism controls where non-sterile
7. Particulate and visible-particle control
8. Container-closure integrity and extractables/leachables assessment
9. Compatibility statement limited to directly studied products
10. In-use, after-opening and puncture stability
11. Manufacturer, site, batch and supply-chain evidence

## Minimum analytical methods

- Composition/assay method
- pH and osmolality
- Sterility/endotoxin where claimed
- Container-closure integrity

## Automatic rejection or quarantine triggers

- Concentration or pH omitted
- Sterile claim supported only by a generic certificate
- Universal compatibility or reconstitution claim

## Release rule

Completion of this schedule permits Product Passport technical review only. It does not establish approval, safety, efficacy, human-use suitability, direct sale or Platinum status.
