# SC09 — Critical Identity or Composition Quarantine

## Scope

Product whose name, molecular identity, composition, concentration or marketed-product relationship is unresolved or materially conflicting.

## Mandatory evidence

1. Direct manufacturer declaration of complete identity or composition
2. Unambiguous proper name and product definition
3. Qualified reference standard
4. Independent orthogonal analytical confirmation
5. Resolution of every known identity conflict or name collision
6. Batch-specific certificate of analysis and raw-data package
7. Manufacturer, site and supply-chain authentication
8. Regulatory-status and marketed-product non-equivalence statement
9. Class-specific evidence after quarantine release

## Minimum analytical methods

- At least two orthogonal identity methods
- Independent laboratory confirmation
- Reference-standard comparison

## Automatic rejection or quarantine triggers

- Vendor assertion without primary or analytical support
- Silent correction of a possible typo or trade name
- Progression to efficacy, safety or commercial review before identity closure

## Release rule

Completion of this schedule permits Product Passport technical review only. It does not establish approval, safety, efficacy, human-use suitability, direct sale or Platinum status.
