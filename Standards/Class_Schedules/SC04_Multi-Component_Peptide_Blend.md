# SC04 — Multi-Component Peptide Blend

## Scope

Two or more peptide actives presented in one vial or finished blend.

## Mandatory evidence

1. Complete identity package for every component under SC01/SC02/SC03 as applicable
2. Declared target ratio and batch-specific measured ratio
3. Total mass balance against label claim
4. Component-specific assay with demonstrated selectivity
5. Blend-specific chromatographic and mass-spectrometric fingerprint
6. Compatibility and interaction assessment
7. Co-lyophilisation or co-formulation development evidence
8. Solid-state and solution degradation study
9. Post-preparation or post-reconstitution stability where relevant
10. Aggregation, precipitation and adsorption assessment
11. Sterility, endotoxin and particulate evidence for sterile presentation
12. Evidence-transfer statement confirming that component evidence does not prove blend efficacy

## Minimum analytical methods

- Component-specific LC-MS
- Component-specific assay
- Ratio and total mass-balance calculation
- Blend stability-indicating method

## Automatic rejection or quarantine triggers

- Any component identity unresolved
- Ratio absent or based only on weighed inputs
- No component-specific assay
- Clinical evidence transferred from individual components to the blend

## Release rule

Completion of this schedule permits Product Passport technical review only. It does not establish approval, safety, efficacy, human-use suitability, direct sale or Platinum status.
