# SC07 — Small Molecule or Biochemical Ancillary

## Scope

Non-peptide chemical, metabolite, cofactor, small-molecule inhibitor or biochemical ancillary product.

## Mandatory evidence

1. Complete chemical name, structure, formula, molecular mass and CAS/UNII where applicable
2. Salt, counter-ion, polymorph, solvate, hydration and redox state
3. Manufacturer, synthesis route and batch
4. Reference standard and traceability
5. Orthogonal identity by NMR, LC-MS, GC-MS, IR or justified methods
6. Assay, related substances and degradation products
7. Residual solvents, elemental impurities and water
8. Chiral purity where applicable
9. Formulation, excipients, container closure and stability
10. Sterility, endotoxin and particles for sterile presentation
11. Regulatory and product-equivalence statement

## Minimum analytical methods

- Orthogonal structural identity
- Assay and impurity profile
- Residual-solvent and elemental-impurity assessment
- Stability-indicating method

## Automatic rejection or quarantine triggers

- Salt, redox state or chemical form omitted
- Identity supported only by catalogue name
- Food or dietary grade presented as sterile-drug suitability

## Release rule

Completion of this schedule permits Product Passport technical review only. It does not establish approval, safety, efficacy, human-use suitability, direct sale or Platinum status.
