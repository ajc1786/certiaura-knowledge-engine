# SC05 — Biological Extract or Heterogeneous Mixture

## Scope

Animal- or tissue-derived extract, hydrolysate, polypeptide mixture or compositionally heterogeneous product.

## Mandatory evidence

1. Species, tissue, donor and geographic source
2. Animal-health, donor-screening and traceability controls
3. Extraction, hydrolysis, purification and fractionation process
4. Viral, prion and transmissible-agent risk assessment
5. Molecular-weight distribution and compositional fingerprint
6. Marker peptides, amino-acid profile and batch-consistency criteria
7. Reference batch or comparator fingerprint
8. Process impurities, residual reagents and microbial controls
9. Biological potency or justified functional assay
10. Container closure, storage, cold chain and stability
11. Sterility, endotoxin and particles for sterile presentation
12. Manufacturer and marketed/reference-product comparability package

## Minimum analytical methods

- Orthogonal compositional fingerprinting
- Molecular-weight distribution
- Marker or potency assay
- Batch-to-batch statistical comparability

## Automatic rejection or quarantine triggers

- Species or tissue source omitted
- Product described as one peptide without evidence
- No batch fingerprint or source-control evidence

## Release rule

Completion of this schedule permits Product Passport technical review only. It does not establish approval, safety, efficacy, human-use suitability, direct sale or Platinum status.
