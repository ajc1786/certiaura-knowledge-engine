# SC08 — Cosmetic, Device, Polymer or Injectable Technology

## Scope

Polymer, dermal-filler-type product, cosmetic injectable, device-led presentation or product whose performance depends on rheology, crosslinking or delivery-device characteristics.

## Mandatory evidence

1. Complete composition, polymer identity and concentration
2. Molecular-weight distribution and crosslinking chemistry
3. Crosslinker residuals and process impurities
4. Rheology, particle size and physical-performance specifications
5. Device, syringe, needle or delivery-system specifications
6. Biocompatibility and local-tolerance evidence
7. Sterility, endotoxin, visible and subvisible particulate evidence
8. Container-closure integrity and extractables/leachables
9. Shelf-life and in-use stability
10. Manufacturer, site and regulated-product status
11. Authenticity and authorised/cleared device comparability

## Minimum analytical methods

- Composition and polymer characterisation
- Rheology/physical-performance testing
- Biocompatibility evidence
- Sterility/endotoxin and container integrity

## Automatic rejection or quarantine triggers

- Composition or crosslinker omitted
- Device or cosmetic status represented as drug approval
- No sterility or biocompatibility evidence for injectable presentation

## Release rule

Completion of this schedule permits Product Passport technical review only. It does not establish approval, safety, efficacy, human-use suitability, direct sale or Platinum status.
