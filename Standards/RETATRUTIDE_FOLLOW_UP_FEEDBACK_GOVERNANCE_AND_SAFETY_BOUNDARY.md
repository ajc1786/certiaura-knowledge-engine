# Retatrutide Follow-Up, Feedback and Amendment Governance and Safety Boundary

Build provenance: CERT-BUILD-0049

This baseline supports controlled records and auditability. It does not diagnose, prescribe, alter dose, advise starting or stopping treatment, replace clinical judgement or certify safety.

Direct identifiers are prohibited. Pseudonymous references must not be reverse-engineered within the package. Urgent routing is locked and cannot be downgraded by acknowledgement, feedback or administrative closure. Every amendment preserves the original record and complete audit chain.
