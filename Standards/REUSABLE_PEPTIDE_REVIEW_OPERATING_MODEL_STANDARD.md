# Reusable Peptide Review Operating Model Standard

Build: `0059`

The reusable operating model carries forward governance mechanics only: role separation, immutable evidence packs, challenge and appeal independence, suspension recovery, periodic reassessment, audit replay, fail-closed routing and exact closure evidence. Peptide-specific biology, indications, safety boundaries, monitoring thresholds and clinical claims must be rebuilt from target-specific evidence for every new peptide.

Cross-peptide clinical equivalence and automatic transfer of Tesamorelin assumptions are prohibited.
