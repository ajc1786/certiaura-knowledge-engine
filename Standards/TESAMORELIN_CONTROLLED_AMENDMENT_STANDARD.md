# Tesamorelin Controlled Amendment Standard

Build: `0058`

Any amendment to the governed Tesamorelin simulation baseline must identify its trigger records, current and proposed versions, changed controls, impact assessment, validation state, rollback plan and named human approvers. Approval is prohibited until validation is complete and rollback is demonstrably available.

Amendment approval applies only to the knowledge-workflow simulation. It cannot amend clinical care, prescribe treatment or create autonomous clinical decision support.
