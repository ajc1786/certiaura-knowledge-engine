# INDEPENDENT LABORATORY QUALIFICATION STANDARD

## Required evidence

- legal entity and physical laboratory address;
- current accreditation scope or equivalent competency evidence;
- named quality manager and technical signatory;
- method-specific capability and instrument list;
- reference-standard control;
- sample receipt, chain-of-custody and retention procedure;
- electronic raw-data and audit-trail controls;
- out-of-specification and deviation procedure;
- proficiency testing or inter-laboratory comparison where available;
- conflict-of-interest declaration;
- subcontracting disclosure.

## Qualification states

| State | Use |
|---|---|
| QUALIFIED | May generate evidence for defined methods within scope |
| CONDITIONALLY QUALIFIED | Limited review use; deficiencies require closure |
| NOT QUALIFIED | Results may not close Product Passport fields |
| SUSPENDED | New data blocked pending investigation |

Accreditation alone does not prove competence for a specific peptide, biologic, mixture or sterile-product method.
