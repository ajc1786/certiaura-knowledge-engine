# Retatrutide Architecture Reuse Decision Standard

Architecture reuse is a governed decision, not an automatic consequence of a successful Retatrutide build. Reusable controls must be separated from Retatrutide-specific scientific content. `APPROVE_REUSE` requires a `PLATINUM_READY` source baseline, no open prerequisites and explicit human approval. `PILOT_ONLY` is mandatory where readiness or peptide-specific adaptation remains incomplete.
