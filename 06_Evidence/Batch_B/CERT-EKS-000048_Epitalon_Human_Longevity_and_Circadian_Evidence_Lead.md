# CERT-EKS-000048 — Epitalon Human Longevity and Circadian Evidence Lead

**Root asset:** `CERT-PKS-000010`  
**Version:** 0.2.0  
**Status:** HUMAN PUBLICATION IDENTITIES PARTIAL — EPITALON / EPITHALAMIN SEPARATION REQUIRED  
**Last Review:** 2026-07-16

## Domain

Retinal, melatonin, telomere, morbidity and mortality claims

## Extracted position

- A human retinitis pigmentosa publication attributed to Epitalon is identifiable.
- Many longevity and mortality claims concern epithalamin, a pineal extract, rather than synthetic Epitalon.
- Direct methods, randomisation, controls and product identity remain insufficiently closed.

## Limitations

- Epithalamin evidence cannot be assigned to Epitalon automatically.
- Older reports require independent direct verification.
- No large independent placebo-controlled longevity trial is established.

## Permitted claims

- Human evidence is sparse, heterogeneous and identity-sensitive.

## Prohibited claims

- Claims that Epitalon reduces human mortality.
- Claims that epithalamin and Epitalon are clinically interchangeable.

**Complete:** No, unless a future build explicitly upgrades the source state.
