## Trial

- TRIUMPH-1
- Phase 3
- 2,339 adults
- Obesity or overweight with at least one weight-related comorbidity
- No diabetes
- Retatrutide 4 mg, 9 mg, 12 mg, or placebo
- Main analysis at 80 weeks

## Efficacy estimand — week 80

| Group | Mean body-weight change |
|---|---:|
| Retatrutide 4 mg | -19.0% |
| Retatrutide 9 mg | -25.9% |
| Retatrutide 12 mg | -28.3% |
| Placebo | -2.2% |

### Weight-loss thresholds — 12 mg

- At least 30% weight loss: **45.3%**
- At least 35% weight loss: **27.2%**
- BMI below 30 at week 80: **65.3%**

## Prespecified 104-week extension

Among 532 participants with baseline BMI ≥35 who completed the main study and tolerated assigned medication:

| Group | Mean body-weight change at week 104 |
|---|---:|
| 4 mg to maximum tolerated dose | -27.9% |
| 9 mg to maximum tolerated dose | -29.5% |
| 12 mg to maximum tolerated dose | -30.3% |
