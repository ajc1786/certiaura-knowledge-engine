# CERT-EKS-000364 — Cagrilintide Safety and Living Evidence Closure

**Root asset:** `CERT-PKS-000013`  
**Evidence type:** Safety, contraindication and current development evidence  
**Grade:** A1/B1 — current official source plus primary clinical evidence  
**Status:** SOURCE CLOSED WITHIN DEFINED SCOPE / LIVING EVIDENCE ACTIVE  
**Last Review:** 2026-07-17

## Controlled conclusion

The dominant reported adverse events are gastrointestinal disorders and administration-site reactions. Amylin-related gastric, nutritional and interaction effects remain formulation- and dose-specific. CagriSema safety and submission status cannot be transferred to unverified cagrilintide powder.
