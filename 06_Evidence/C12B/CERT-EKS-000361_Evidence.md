# CERT-EKS-000361 — Retatrutide Safety and Living Evidence Closure

**Root asset:** `CERT-PKS-000001`  
**Evidence type:** Safety, contraindication and current development evidence  
**Grade:** A1/B1 — current official source plus primary clinical evidence  
**Status:** SOURCE CLOSED WITHIN DEFINED SCOPE / LIVING EVIDENCE ACTIVE  
**Last Review:** 2026-07-17

## Controlled conclusion

Published retatrutide studies report predominantly gastrointestinal adverse events, with dose-related heart-rate increases in the phase 2 obesity trial. Longer-term cardiovascular, gallbladder, pancreatic, retinal, reproductive and rare-event risks remain subject to ongoing phase 3 and post-development evaluation.

## Sources

- **Retatrutide phase 2 obesity trial** — Primary randomised phase 2 human evidence; Published 2023; https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/37366315/

- **TRIUMPH-3 cardiovascular-disease registry** — Current authoritative trial registry; Active, not recruiting; last update 2026-05-22; https://clinicaltrials.gov/study/NCT05882045
- **TRIUMPH-5 retatrutide-versus-tirzepatide registry** — Current authoritative trial registry; Active, not recruiting; last update 2026-04-13; https://clinicaltrials.gov/study/NCT06662383
