# CERT-EKS-000372 — Cibinetide / ARA-290 Pivotal Human Evidence Source Closure

**Root asset:** `CERT-PKS-000054`  
**Evidence type:** Primary human efficacy and translation evidence  
**Grade:** B1 — pivotal, major randomised or defined phase 2/3 human evidence  
**Status:** SOURCE CLOSED WITHIN DEFINED SCOPE / LIVING EVIDENCE ACTIVE  
**Last Review:** 2026-07-17

## Controlled conclusion

Small phase 2 studies evaluated ARA-290/cibinetide in sarcoidosis-associated small-fibre neuropathy, type 2 diabetes with neuropathic symptoms and diabetic macular oedema. These studies were limited in size and duration and do not establish a broadly authorised neuropathy, inflammatory or regenerative therapy.

## Sources

- **Cibinetide phase 2b sarcoidosis small-fibre-loss trial** — Primary randomised phase 2b human evidence; Published 2017; https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/28475703/

- **Cibinetide diabetic macular oedema phase 2 study** — Primary phase 2 human evidence; Published 2020; https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/32674280/
