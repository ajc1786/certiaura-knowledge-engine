# CERT-EKS-000363 — Cagrilintide Pivotal Human Evidence Source Closure

**Root asset:** `CERT-PKS-000013`  
**Evidence type:** Primary human efficacy and translation evidence  
**Grade:** B1 — pivotal, major randomised or defined phase 2/3 human evidence  
**Status:** SOURCE CLOSED WITHIN DEFINED SCOPE / LIVING EVIDENCE ACTIVE  
**Last Review:** 2026-07-17

## Controlled conclusion

A 706-participant phase 2 dose-finding trial established cagrilintide monotherapy weight-management evidence. REDEFINE 1 subsequently included cagrilintide 2.4 mg as an active monotherapy arm alongside CagriSema, semaglutide and placebo. Separate monotherapy phase 3 RENEW studies were initiated.

## Sources

- **Cagrilintide phase 2 dose-finding trial** — Primary randomised phase 2 human evidence; Published 2021; https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/34798060/
- **REDEFINE 1 phase 3 publication** — Primary randomised phase 3 evidence including cagrilintide arm; Published 2025; https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/40544433/
