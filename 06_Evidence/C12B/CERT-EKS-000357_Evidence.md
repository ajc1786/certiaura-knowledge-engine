# CERT-EKS-000357 — Somatropin Pivotal Human Evidence Source Closure

**Root asset:** `CERT-PKS-000020`  
**Evidence type:** Primary human efficacy and translation evidence  
**Grade:** B1 — pivotal, major randomised or defined phase 2/3 human evidence  
**Status:** SOURCE CLOSED WITHIN DEFINED SCOPE / LIVING EVIDENCE ACTIVE  
**Last Review:** 2026-07-17

## Controlled conclusion

Randomised paediatric growth-hormone-deficiency trials show that authentic recombinant somatropin can improve growth outcomes, while controlled adult studies support replacement effects in confirmed growth-hormone deficiency. These findings are indication-, population-, product- and monitoring-specific and do not validate catalogue vials.

## Sources

- **Four-year randomised daily versus three-times-weekly somatropin trial** — Randomised paediatric human evidence; Published 1996; https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/8626839/
- **Two-year placebo-controlled somatropin dose-response study in young adults** — Randomised adult human evidence; Published 2003; https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/14602761/

## Restrictions

- Authorised, sponsor and trial-material evidence does not establish catalogue-material equivalence.
- No personal dosing, injection or reconstitution protocol is created.
- The object is not an exhaustive systematic review.
