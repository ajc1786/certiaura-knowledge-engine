# CERT-EKS-000365 — Mazdutide Product, Identity and Regulatory Source Closure

**Root asset:** `CERT-PKS-000014`

## Controlled conclusion

China's NMPA approved an exact mazdutide finished product for chronic weight management in June 2025 and for glycaemic control in adults with type 2 diabetes in September 2025. The approvals are product-, manufacturer-, dose-, formulation- and jurisdiction-specific. Catalogue MDT5 and MDT10 powders are not that authorised product.
