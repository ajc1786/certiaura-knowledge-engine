# CERT-EKS-000366 — Mazdutide Pivotal Human Evidence Source Closure

**Root asset:** `CERT-PKS-000014`  
**Evidence type:** Primary human efficacy and translation evidence  
**Grade:** B1 — pivotal, major randomised or defined phase 2/3 human evidence  
**Status:** SOURCE CLOSED WITHIN DEFINED SCOPE / LIVING EVIDENCE ACTIVE  
**Last Review:** 2026-07-17

## Controlled conclusion

GLORY-1 established phase 3 weight-management evidence for 4 mg and 6 mg mazdutide in Chinese adults. DREAMS-1 established phase 3 monotherapy evidence in type 2 diabetes. GLORY-2 reported 9 mg evidence in 2026, while the 9 mg supplementary application remained under Chinese regulatory review.

## Sources

- **GLORY-1 phase 3 trial** — Primary randomised phase 3 weight-management evidence; Published 2025; https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/40421736/
- **DREAMS-1 phase 3 trial** — Primary randomised phase 3 diabetes evidence; Published online 2025; issue 2026; https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/41407859/
- **GLORY-2 9 mg phase 3 trial** — Primary randomised phase 3 weight-management evidence; Published 2026; https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/42251595/
