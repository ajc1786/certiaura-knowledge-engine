# CERT-EKS-000358 — Somatropin Safety and Living Evidence Closure

**Root asset:** `CERT-PKS-000020`  
**Evidence type:** Safety, contraindication and current development evidence  
**Grade:** A1/B1 — current official source plus primary clinical evidence  
**Status:** SOURCE CLOSED WITHIN DEFINED SCOPE / LIVING EVIDENCE ACTIVE  
**Last Review:** 2026-07-17

## Controlled conclusion

Current somatropin controls include acute critical illness mortality, active malignancy, glucose intolerance, intracranial hypertension, fluid retention, slipped capital femoral epiphysis, scoliosis progression, pancreatitis and specific Prader-Willi syndrome risks. Growth-hormone use remains subject to current anti-doping controls.
