# CERT-EKS-000369 — Survodutide Pivotal Human Evidence Source Closure

**Root asset:** `CERT-PKS-000015`  
**Evidence type:** Primary human efficacy and translation evidence  
**Grade:** B1 — pivotal, major randomised or defined phase 2/3 human evidence  
**Status:** SOURCE CLOSED WITHIN DEFINED SCOPE / LIVING EVIDENCE ACTIVE  
**Last Review:** 2026-07-17

## Controlled conclusion

Phase 3 SYNCHRONIZE-1 evaluated survodutide in adults with obesity without diabetes. A phase 2 biopsy-based MASH trial reported improvement in MASH without worsening fibrosis. Other phase 3 studies cover diabetes, cardiovascular safety, China and metabolic liver disease.

## Sources

- **SYNCHRONIZE-1 phase 3 obesity publication** — Primary randomised phase 3 human evidence; Published 2026-06; https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/42253238/
- **Survodutide phase 2 MASH and fibrosis trial** — Primary randomised biopsy-based phase 2 evidence; Published 2024; https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/38847460/

- **SYNCHRONIZE-2 diabetes trial** — Current authoritative phase 3 registry; Last update 2026-04-24; https://clinicaltrials.gov/study/NCT06066528
