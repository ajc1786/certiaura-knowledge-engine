# CERT-EKS-000370 — Survodutide Safety and Living Evidence Closure

**Root asset:** `CERT-PKS-000015`  
**Evidence type:** Safety, contraindication and current development evidence  
**Grade:** A1/B1 — current official source plus primary clinical evidence  
**Status:** SOURCE CLOSED WITHIN DEFINED SCOPE / LIVING EVIDENCE ACTIVE  
**Last Review:** 2026-07-17

## Controlled conclusion

The published programme reports frequent gastrointestinal adverse events, especially nausea, diarrhoea and vomiting. Long-term cardiovascular, hepatic, gallbladder, pancreatic, nutritional and rare-event risks remain under active phase 3 evaluation. Survodutide is not represented as an approved medicine in this source packet.
