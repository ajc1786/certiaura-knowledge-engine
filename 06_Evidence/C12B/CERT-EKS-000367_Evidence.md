# CERT-EKS-000367 — Mazdutide Safety and Living Evidence Closure

**Root asset:** `CERT-PKS-000014`  
**Evidence type:** Safety, contraindication and current development evidence  
**Grade:** A1/B1 — current official source plus primary clinical evidence  
**Status:** SOURCE CLOSED WITHIN DEFINED SCOPE / LIVING EVIDENCE ACTIVE  
**Last Review:** 2026-07-17

## Controlled conclusion

The phase 3 programme reports predominantly gastrointestinal adverse events, including nausea, vomiting and diarrhoea. Product-specific titration, hepatic, gallbladder, pancreatic, cardiovascular and rare-event controls require the exact Chinese label and ongoing evidence. No non-Chinese authorisation is inferred.
