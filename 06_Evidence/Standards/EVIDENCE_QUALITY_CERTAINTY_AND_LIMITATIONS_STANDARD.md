# Evidence Quality, Certainty and Limitations Standard

**UAI:** CERT-EKS-000756
**Version:** 1.0.0

Evidence quality is recorded separately from relevance and from certainty of a specific conclusion. Reviewers must assess study design, risk of bias, directness, precision, consistency, publication status, conflicts, population applicability and reporting limitations.

Permitted certainty states are `HIGH`, `MODERATE`, `LOW`, `VERY_LOW`, `INSUFFICIENT` and `NOT_APPLICABLE`. Every rating requires a concise rationale and explicit limitations. A high-quality source does not automatically justify a broad or causal claim.
