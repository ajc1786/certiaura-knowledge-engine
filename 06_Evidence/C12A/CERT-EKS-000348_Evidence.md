# CERT-EKS-000348 — Teriparatide Pivotal Human Evidence Source Closure

**Root asset:** `CERT-PKS-000035`  
**Evidence type:** Primary human efficacy and translation evidence  
**Grade:** B1 — pivotal or major randomised human evidence  
**Status:** SOURCE CLOSED WITHIN DEFINED SCOPE / LIVING EVIDENCE ACTIVE  
**Last Review:** 2026-07-16

## Controlled conclusion

The pivotal fracture-prevention trial randomised 1,637 postmenopausal women with osteoporosis to placebo or teriparatide and established product-specific vertebral and non-vertebral fracture evidence. That trial does not validate TER10 quality or use.

## Sources

- **Effect of parathyroid hormone (1-34) on fractures and bone mineral density** — Pivotal randomised controlled trial; Published 2001; https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/11346808/
