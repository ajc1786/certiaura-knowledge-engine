# CERT-EKS-000345 — Liraglutide Pivotal Human Evidence Source Closure

**Root asset:** `CERT-PKS-000016`  
**Evidence type:** Primary human efficacy and translation evidence  
**Grade:** B1 — pivotal or major randomised human evidence  
**Status:** SOURCE CLOSED WITHIN DEFINED SCOPE / LIVING EVIDENCE ACTIVE  
**Last Review:** 2026-07-16

## Controlled conclusion

The SCALE obesity trial established weight-management evidence for sponsor-controlled liraglutide 3.0 mg. LEADER established cardiovascular-outcome evidence for a defined liraglutide diabetes product and population. Evidence must remain product, dose, route and indication specific.

## Sources

- **A randomised controlled trial of liraglutide 3.0 mg in weight management** — SCALE pivotal randomised controlled trial; Published 2015; https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/26132939/
- **Liraglutide and cardiovascular outcomes in type 2 diabetes** — LEADER cardiovascular-outcome trial; Published 2016; https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/27295427/

## Translation restrictions

- Authorised finished-product evidence does not establish catalogue-vial equivalence.
- Trial-material evidence does not certify supplier identity, quality or sterility.
- No dosing, injection or reconstitution protocol is created.
- The object is not an exhaustive systematic review.
