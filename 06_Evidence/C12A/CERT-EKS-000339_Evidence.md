# CERT-EKS-000339 — Semaglutide Pivotal Human Evidence Source Closure

**Root asset:** `CERT-PKS-000011`  
**Evidence type:** Primary human efficacy and translation evidence  
**Grade:** B1 — pivotal or major randomised human evidence  
**Status:** SOURCE CLOSED WITHIN DEFINED SCOPE / LIVING EVIDENCE ACTIVE  
**Last Review:** 2026-07-16

## Controlled conclusion

STEP 1 established substantial weight reduction with sponsor-controlled semaglutide 2.4 mg injection in adults with overweight or obesity. SELECT established cardiovascular outcome evidence in adults with established cardiovascular disease and overweight or obesity without diabetes. These results are not transferable to supplier powders.

## Sources

- **Once-weekly semaglutide in adults with overweight or obesity** — STEP 1 pivotal randomised controlled trial; Published 2021; https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/33567185/
- **Semaglutide and cardiovascular outcomes in obesity without diabetes** — SELECT cardiovascular-outcome trial; Published 2023; https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/37952131/
