# CERT-EKS-000336 — Tesamorelin Pivotal Human Evidence Source Closure

**Root asset:** `CERT-PKS-000003`  
**Evidence type:** Primary human efficacy and translation evidence  
**Grade:** B1 — pivotal or major randomised human evidence  
**Status:** SOURCE CLOSED WITHIN DEFINED SCOPE / LIVING EVIDENCE ACTIVE  
**Last Review:** 2026-07-16

## Controlled conclusion

Two randomised placebo-controlled programmes in people with HIV and excess abdominal fat demonstrated reductions in visceral adipose tissue. The pivotal evidence is tied to protocol-defined tesamorelin material, populations, duration and clinical monitoring.

## Sources

- **Metabolic effects of a growth hormone-releasing factor in patients with HIV** — Pivotal randomised controlled trial; Published 2007; https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/18057338/
- **Effects of tesamorelin in HIV-infected patients with abdominal fat accumulation** — Randomised controlled trial with safety extension; Published 2010; https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/20101189/
- **Tesamorelin in people with HIV on integrase inhibitors** — Recent randomised controlled evidence; Published 2024; https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/38905488/
