# CERT-EKS-000337 — Tesamorelin Safety, Contraindication and Living Evidence Closure

**Root asset:** `CERT-PKS-000003`  
**Evidence type:** Safety, contraindication and current-status evidence  
**Grade:** A1/B1 — current label plus primary clinical evidence  
**Status:** SOURCE CLOSED WITHIN DEFINED SCOPE / LIVING EVIDENCE ACTIVE  
**Last Review:** 2026-07-16

## Controlled conclusion

Current controls include active malignancy contraindication, IGF-1 elevation, glucose intolerance/diabetes risk, hypersensitivity, fluid retention and injection-site reactions. Long-term cardiovascular safety has not been established.
