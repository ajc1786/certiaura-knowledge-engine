# CERT-EKS-000349 — Teriparatide Safety, Contraindication and Living Evidence Closure

**Root asset:** `CERT-PKS-000035`  
**Evidence type:** Safety, contraindication and current-status evidence  
**Grade:** A1/B1 — current label plus primary clinical evidence  
**Status:** SOURCE CLOSED WITHIN DEFINED SCOPE / LIVING EVIDENCE ACTIVE  
**Last Review:** 2026-07-16

## Controlled conclusion

Current controls include avoidance in patients at increased baseline osteosarcoma risk, hypercalcaemia, orthostatic hypotension, urolithiasis considerations and product-specific handling. Treatment duration and sequencing belong to licensed clinical care, not supplier powder.
