# CERT-EKS-000352 — Afamelanotide / Melanotan I Safety, Contraindication and Living Evidence Closure

**Root asset:** `CERT-PKS-000079`  
**Evidence type:** Safety, contraindication and current-status evidence  
**Grade:** A1/B1 — current label plus primary clinical evidence  
**Status:** SOURCE CLOSED WITHIN DEFINED SCOPE / LIVING EVIDENCE ACTIVE  
**Last Review:** 2026-07-16

## Controlled conclusion

Current controls include trained implantation, hypersensitivity/anaphylaxis, insertion-site monitoring, skin pigmentation changes and periodic full-body skin examination. The authorised evidence does not support unsupervised powder use or cosmetic tanning.
