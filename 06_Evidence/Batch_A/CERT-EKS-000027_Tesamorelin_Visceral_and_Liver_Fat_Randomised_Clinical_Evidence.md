## Publication

**Title:** Effect of tesamorelin on visceral fat and liver fat in HIV-infected patients with abdominal fat accumulation: a randomized clinical trial  
**Journal:** JAMA  
**Year:** 2014  
**DOI:** `10.1001/jama.2014.8334`  
**PMID:** `25038357`

## Study design

Randomised clinical trial evaluating visceral adipose tissue and hepatic fat in HIV-infected adults with abdominal fat accumulation.

## Population or model

Adults living with HIV and abdominal fat accumulation.

## Intervention or exposure

Tesamorelin versus placebo; detailed treatment schedule remains tied to the primary article and is not reproduced as guidance.

> This is a historical study-design record, not a recommended administration protocol.

## Extracted outcomes

- The publication supports study of both visceral and hepatic fat outcomes.
- Precise effect sizes remain open pending complete direct extraction.

## Limitations

- Full primary article was not captured in Build 0018.
- Population-specific evidence does not validate general anti-obesity or anti-ageing use.

## Permitted claims

- Tesamorelin has randomised human evidence involving visceral and liver-fat outcomes in a defined HIV population.

## Prohibited claims

- Unqualified liver-fat treatment claims.
- Transfer to non-HIV populations without separate evidence.
