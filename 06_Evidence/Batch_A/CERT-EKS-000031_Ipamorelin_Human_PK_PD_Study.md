# CERT-EKS-000031 — Ipamorelin Human PK-PD Study

**Root asset:** `CERT-PKS-000005`  
**Version:** 1.0.0  
**Status:** CLOSED FOR PRIMARY ABSTRACT EXTRACTION  
**Last Review:** 2026-07-16

## Publication

**Title:** Pharmacokinetic-pharmacodynamic modeling of ipamorelin, a growth hormone releasing peptide, in human volunteers  
**Journal:** Pharmaceutical Research  
**Year:** 1999  
**DOI:** `10.1023/A:1018955126402`  
**PMID:** `10496658`

## Study design

Dose-escalation trial using five intravenous infusion rates, with eight healthy male subjects at each level.

## Population or model

Healthy male volunteers.

## Intervention or exposure

Fifteen-minute intravenous infusions across five escalating study-dose levels.

> This is a historical study-design record, not a recommended administration protocol.

## Extracted outcomes

- PK was dose proportional.
- Terminal half-life was approximately 2 hours.
- Clearance was reported as 0.078 L/h/kg and steady-state volume as 0.22 L/kg.
- GH response showed a single peak at approximately 0.67 hours followed by decline.
- Ipamorelin stimulated GH release at all tested study levels.

## Limitations

- Male healthy volunteers.
- Intravenous experimental design.
- PK/PD modelling study, not efficacy evidence.
- No chronic-use safety conclusion.

## Permitted claims

- Ipamorelin has direct human PK/PD evidence showing a short half-life and episodic GH response.

## Prohibited claims

- Chronic wellness, muscle-gain or fat-loss efficacy claims.
- Self-administration conversion from study doses.
