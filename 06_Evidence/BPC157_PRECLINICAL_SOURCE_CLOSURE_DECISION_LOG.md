## Decision

No evidence object is upgraded to **CLOSED FOR CORE EXTRACTION** merely because:

- its title is known;
- it appears in later reviews;
- a DOI is known;
- an abstract or secondary description exists.

## Why this matters

Precise study-design and outcome claims require the primary article.

This is especially important for:

- animal numbers;
- allocation;
- dose;
- route;
- treatment timing;
- endpoint selection;
- statistical methods;
- adverse findings;
- pathology;
- conflicts and funding.
