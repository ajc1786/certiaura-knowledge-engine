## Reported late-stage findings

The report described a 40-week late-stage trial in people with type 2 diabetes inadequately controlled with diet and exercise alone.

Reported results:

- Mean HbA1c reduction across retatrutide doses: **1.7% to 2.0%**
- Placebo mean HbA1c reduction: **0.8%**
- Weight loss: up to **15.3%**, using the reported analysis including intercurrent events

Reported highest-dose adverse-event frequencies:

- Nausea: **26.5%**
- Diarrhoea: **22.8%**
- Vomiting: **17.6%**
