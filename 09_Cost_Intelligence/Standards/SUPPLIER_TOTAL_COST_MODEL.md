# Supplier Total Cost Model

**Build:** CERT-BUILD-0035J  
**UAI:** UAI_ALLOCATION_REQUIRED

## Objective

Provide a consistent total-cost comparison without allowing the model to make a sourcing award.

## Required cost layers

1. Purchase cost: unit price, quantity, minimum order, rebates and committed volume.
2. Landed cost: freight, duty, taxes, brokerage and handling.
3. Assurance cost: due diligence, audit, testing, evidence review and monitoring.
4. Operational cost: storage, cold chain, finance, administration and system integration.
5. Risk-adjusted cost: delay, failure, rejection, replacement, continuity stock and switching.
6. Exit cost: termination, transition, revalidation and residual inventory.

## Calculation control

Every model must record currency, period, source date, assumptions, exclusions, confidence level and reviewer. Outputs are estimates and decision support. A calculated lowest total cost cannot override assurance, safety, integrity, quality or resilience blockers.
