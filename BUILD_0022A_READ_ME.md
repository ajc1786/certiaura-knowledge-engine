## Critical identity controls

- Somatropin is recombinant 191-amino-acid human growth hormone.
- Sermorelin is GHRH (1-29) and is not modified GRF (1-29).
- Modified GRF (1-29) is commonly mislabelled as “CJC-1295 without DAC”.
- CJC-1295 with DAC remains a separate existing asset.
- GHRP-2, GHRP-6, Hexarelin and Ipamorelin are distinct ghrelin-receptor agonists.
- Supplier vials do not inherit the identity, quality or approval of authorised medicines.
