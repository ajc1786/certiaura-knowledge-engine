## Critical controls

- “Humanin” does not specify native sequence, analogue, salt, termini or formulation.
- FOXO4-DRI is a large identity-sensitive retro-inverso D-peptide construct; the catalogue shorthand “FOXO4” does not prove that exact construct.
- FOXO4-DRI evidence is preclinical and does not establish human senolytic, anti-ageing or rejuvenation efficacy.
- N-Acetyl Epitalon Amidate is chemically distinct from Epitalon and cannot inherit its evidence.
- Vilon and Testagen require exact sequence, stereochemistry, termini and reference-standard verification.
- Regional short-peptide literature does not establish an authorised medicine, validated anti-ageing protocol or supplier-product equivalence.
- No senolytic, longevity, fertility, endocrine, dosing or reconstitution protocol is created.
