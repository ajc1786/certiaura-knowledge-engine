## Critical controls

- “Bac.water” does not prove USP identity, 0.9% benzyl alcohol, sterility, pyrogen status, multidose suitability or compatibility.
- Acetic acid concentration and pH are absent; no diluent use is permitted from the catalogue name alone.
- Botulinum toxin serotype, strain, complex, brand, manufacturer and potency system are absent. Botulinum units are product specific.
- Hyaluronic acid molecular weight, source, crosslinking, rheology, endotoxin, route and device status are absent.
- Alprostadil formulation, route, excipients and authorised-product equivalence are absent.
- Lemon Bottle is a cosmetic/injectable mixture, not a peptide. The catalogue has conflicting price and composition records.
- No reconstitution, cosmetic injection, dermal filler, botulinum, erectile-dysfunction, fat-dissolving or self-administration protocol is created.
