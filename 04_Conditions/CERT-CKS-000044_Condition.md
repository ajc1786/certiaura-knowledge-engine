# CERT-CKS-000044 — Acquired Generalised Hypoactive Sexual Desire Disorder

**Status:** Foundation condition object  
**Last Review:** 2026-07-16

## Summary

Low sexual desire causing marked distress or interpersonal difficulty in a defined clinical population and not explained by excluded causes.

## Boundary

This condition object does not convert low desire, tanning goals or sexual-performance goals into a self-treatment indication.
