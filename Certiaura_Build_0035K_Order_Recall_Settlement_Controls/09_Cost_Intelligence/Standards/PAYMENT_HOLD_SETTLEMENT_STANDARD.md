# Payment Hold and Settlement Standard

Payment controls protect cash and recovery rights without overriding quality or safety decisions.

Mandatory controls:

- holds created for critical incidents, active recalls, unresolved non-conformance, quantity disputes and missing required evidence;
- gross exposure, recoverable amounts, returned stock, credits, taxes, fees and residual loss recorded separately;
- no automatic hold release, credit approval, write-off or settlement;
- independent commercial and finance approval for material settlements;
- continuing obligations, admissions, confidentiality and legal reservations recorded;
- settlement closure does not automatically close safety, quality, passport, supplier or marketplace records.
