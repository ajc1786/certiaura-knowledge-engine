# Fulfilment, Cold-Chain and Goods Receipt Standard

- Chain-of-custody events must be complete and sequential.
- Storage requirements come from the approved product/batch record, not supplier marketing text.
- Missing, impossible or out-of-range readings trigger quarantine and assessment.
- A temperature excursion is not accepted by default because product appearance is satisfactory.
- Receipt identity must reconcile supplier, order, Product Passport™, batch, quantity and packaging.
- Rejection, destruction, return or conditional acceptance requires named human disposition and evidence.
