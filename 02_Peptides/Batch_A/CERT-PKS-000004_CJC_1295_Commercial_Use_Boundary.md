# CJC-1295 COMMERCIAL USE BOUNDARY

## Knowledge content

Permitted:

- identity and mechanism education;
- evidence summaries;
- safety and monitoring education;
- dated regulatory-status reporting;
- cost and Product Passport™ integration after source validation.

## Prohibited implication

Do not imply:

- universal approval;
- validated self-administration;
- guaranteed purity;
- equivalence between supplier products and studied materials;
- validated universal dosing.

## Direct sale

Any future direct-sale route requires current jurisdiction-specific legal, regulatory and product-quality closure.
