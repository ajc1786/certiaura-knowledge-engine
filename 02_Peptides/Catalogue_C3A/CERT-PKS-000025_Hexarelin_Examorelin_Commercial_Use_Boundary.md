# HEXARELIN / EXAMORELIN COMMERCIAL USE BOUNDARY

## Permitted

- scientific and regulatory education;
- evidence-maturity comparison;
- supplier price and Product Passport™ gap reporting;
- authorised-product links where lawful and accurate.

## Not permitted

- presenting unverified powder as an authorised medicine;
- anti-ageing or performance claims without appropriate evidence;
- self-administration, cycling or reconstitution protocols;
- guaranteed purity, potency or sterility.

## Future direct sale

Requires product-specific legal, regulatory, manufacturing, quality and jurisdiction closure.
