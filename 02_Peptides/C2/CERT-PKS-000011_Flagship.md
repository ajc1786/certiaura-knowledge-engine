## Authorised-product boundary

Semaglutide is used in multiple exact authorised products, formulations and routes. The current United States WEGOVY label includes injection and tablets with product- and indication-specific controls. Catalogue SM lyophilised vials are not WEGOVY, OZEMPIC, RYBELSUS or any authorised formulation.

## Pivotal human evidence

STEP 1 established substantial weight reduction with sponsor-controlled semaglutide 2.4 mg injection in adults with overweight or obesity. SELECT established cardiovascular outcome evidence in adults with established cardiovascular disease and overweight or obesity without diabetes. These results are not transferable to supplier powders.

## Current safety boundary

Current label controls include thyroid C-cell tumour warning, pancreatitis, gallbladder disease, hypoglycaemia in relevant combinations, acute kidney injury from volume depletion, severe gastrointestinal reactions, hypersensitivity, diabetic-retinopathy complications, heart-rate increase and pulmonary aspiration during anaesthesia or deep sedation.
