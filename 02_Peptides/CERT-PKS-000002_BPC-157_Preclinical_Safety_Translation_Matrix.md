# BPC-157 PRECLINICAL SAFETY TRANSLATION MATRIX

| Evidence domain | Canonical asset | What it can support | What it cannot support |
|---|---|---|---|
| Formal preclinical toxicology publication | CERT-EKS-000019 | Existence of an animal toxicology programme | Long-term human safety |
| Animal PK / ADME | CERT-EKS-000020 | Species-specific disposition and exposure | Validated human dose |
| VEGFR2 / angiogenic signalling | CERT-EKS-000024 | Experimental signalling domain | Proof of cancer causation or safety |
| Human safety insufficiency | CERT-SKS-000006 | Explicit evidence-gap control | A specific adverse-event incidence |
| Long-term angiogenic uncertainty | CERT-SKS-000007 | Mechanistic uncertainty domain | Claim that BPC-157 causes cancer |

## Translation rule

A favourable animal toxicology result does not automatically establish:

- human tolerability;
- reproductive safety;
- carcinogenic safety;
- interaction safety;
- product sterility;
- product identity;
- safe chronic use.

## Product-quality separation

Even a complete preclinical safety package for a defined test article would not validate an unrelated online or grey-market product.
