# HGH FRAGMENT 176-191 COMMERCIAL USE BOUNDARY

## Permitted CertiAura activity

- evidence and regulatory-status education;
- supplier-price comparison;
- Product Passport™ gap assessment;
- licensed-product resource links where lawful;
- marketplace controls after identity and legal review.

## Prohibited implication

Do not imply:

- that an unverified powder is an authorised medicine;
- universal legal human use;
- validated self-administration;
- guaranteed purity, sterility or potency;
- automatic transfer of trial outcomes to a catalogue product.

## Direct sale

Any future direct-sale route requires product-specific and jurisdiction-specific legal, regulatory and quality closure.
