# ACE-031 COMMERCIAL USE BOUNDARY

## Permitted

- identity and mechanism education;
- evidence maturity and safety reporting;
- anti-doping warnings;
- catalogue price and Product Passport™ gap reporting.

## Prohibited implication

- authorised medicine or approved treatment;
- established human muscle-growth or performance efficacy;
- self-administration, reconstitution or cycling guidance;
- guaranteed sequence, conjugation, potency, purity or sterility.

## Future direct sale

Requires legal, regulatory, anti-doping, manufacturing, quality and product-identity closure.
