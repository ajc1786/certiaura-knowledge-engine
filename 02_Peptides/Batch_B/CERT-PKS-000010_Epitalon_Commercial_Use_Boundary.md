# EPITALON COMMERCIAL USE BOUNDARY

## Permitted knowledge activity

- identity and mechanism education;
- evidence and safety summaries;
- regulatory-status reporting;
- Product Passport™ and cost integration after validation.

## Do not imply

- universal approval;
- validated self-administration;
- guaranteed product purity or sterility;
- evidence transfer from a different peptide or formulation;
- validated universal dosing.

## Direct sale

Any future direct-sale route requires jurisdiction-specific legal, regulatory and product-quality closure.
