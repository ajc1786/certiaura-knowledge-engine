# BPC-157 SAFETY EVIDENCE MATRIX

| Safety domain | Safety asset | Current interpretation |
|---|---|---|
| Human safety evidence insufficiency | CERT-SKS-000006 | Long-term human safety is not adequately established |
| Long-term angiogenic risk uncertainty | CERT-SKS-000007 | Mechanistic uncertainty requiring investigation; not evidence of causal cancer risk |
| Product quality / sourcing | Future Product Passport / marketplace controls | Compound identity and purity cannot be assumed from an online product label |
| Route and administration safety | Open evidence gap | No universal Certiaura administration protocol is established |

## Rule

Absence of a proven harm signal is not proof of safety.

Preclinical toxicology is not equivalent to long-term human safety.
