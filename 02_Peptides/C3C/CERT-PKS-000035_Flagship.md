# CERT-PKS-000035 — Teriparatide

**Tier:** T1 Primary-Source Closure Candidate

## Authorised-product boundary

Teriparatide is recombinant or synthetic human parathyroid hormone (1-34) used in multiple exact authorised products, including FORTEO and FORSTEO and authorised generic/biosimilar contexts. TER10 supplier powder is not an authorised prefilled pen, does not inherit its excipients or delivery system and has no demonstrated comparability.

## Pivotal human evidence

The pivotal fracture-prevention trial randomised 1,637 postmenopausal women with osteoporosis to placebo or teriparatide and established product-specific vertebral and non-vertebral fracture evidence. That trial does not validate TER10 quality or use.

## Current safety boundary

Current controls include avoidance in patients at increased baseline osteosarcoma risk, hypercalcaemia, orthostatic hypotension, urolithiasis considerations and product-specific handling. Treatment duration and sequencing belong to licensed clinical care, not supplier powder.
