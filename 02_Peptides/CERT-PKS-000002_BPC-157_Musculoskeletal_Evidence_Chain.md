# BPC-157 MUSCULOSKELETAL EVIDENCE CHAIN

## Preclinical chain

| Layer | Evidence asset | Current role |
|---|---|---|
| Rat Achilles tendon injury | CERT-EKS-000015 | Injury-model evidence lead |
| Tendon outgrowth / migration | CERT-EKS-000016 | Mechanistic evidence; primary access blocked |
| Rat ligament injury | CERT-EKS-000017 | Injury-model evidence lead |
| Tendon fibroblast GHR signalling | CERT-EKS-000023 | Directly extracted mechanism |

## What the chain supports

- A coherent preclinical musculoskeletal research programme exists.
- Multiple animal and cell-based domains investigate tendon or ligament biology.
- Some mechanisms have been directly extracted.

## What the chain does not support

- Confirmatory human tendon or ligament efficacy
- A validated clinical rehabilitation adjunct
- A validated injection site, dose, frequency or cycle
- Replacement of physiotherapy, surgery or licensed care
