# RETATRUTIDE COMMERCIAL USE BOUNDARY

**Review date:** 2026-07-15

## Knowledge platform

Permitted:

- evidence summaries;
- trial-status reporting;
- source-status reporting;
- regulatory-status reporting;
- safety and monitoring education.

## Prohibited implication

Do not imply that:

- late-stage trial success equals regulatory approval;
- sponsor intent to submit equals an accepted application;
- online availability equals lawful medicinal supply;
- a supplier certificate proves regulatory approval;
- research-use labelling creates a universal human-use sales route.

## Marketplace

A listing, affiliate link or Product Passport™ must not imply:

- FDA approval;
- MHRA authorisation;
- EMA authorisation;
- universal lawful human use.

## Direct sale

Any future direct-sale route requires jurisdiction-specific legal and regulatory closure.

## Athlete-facing use

Do not state that retatrutide is permitted under anti-doping rules without a current authoritative check.
