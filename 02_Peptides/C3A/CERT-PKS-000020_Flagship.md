**Tier:** T1 Primary-Source Closure Candidate

## Product or regulatory boundary

GENOTROPIN is an exact recombinant human growth hormone biological product with defined indications, formulations, manufacturers, delivery systems and potency. The current United States label was updated on 30 April 2026. Catalogue H-series vials do not inherit GENOTROPIN, NORDITROPIN or another licensed somatropin identity.

## Pivotal human evidence

Randomised paediatric growth-hormone-deficiency trials show that authentic recombinant somatropin can improve growth outcomes, while controlled adult studies support replacement effects in confirmed growth-hormone deficiency. These findings are indication-, population-, product- and monitoring-specific and do not validate catalogue vials.

## Current safety boundary

Current somatropin controls include acute critical illness mortality, active malignancy, glucose intolerance, intracranial hypertension, fluid retention, slipped capital femoral epiphysis, scoliosis progression, pancreatitis and specific Prader-Willi syndrome risks. Growth-hormone use remains subject to current anti-doping controls.
