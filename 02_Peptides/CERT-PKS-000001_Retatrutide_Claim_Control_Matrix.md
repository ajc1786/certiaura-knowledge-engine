## Detailed wording controls

### RET-CLM-001

**Claim:** Retatrutide acts across GLP-1, GIP and glucagon receptor pathways.

**Allowed wording:** Describe as receptor agonist mechanism supported by primary translational evidence.

**Disallowed / caution:** Do not imply every downstream clinical effect is proven solely by receptor activity.

### RET-CLM-002

**Claim:** The phase 2 obesity trial reported 24.2% mean weight reduction at week 48 in the 12 mg group.

**Allowed wording:** State the dose, week and trial context.

**Disallowed / caution:** Do not present as a guaranteed individual outcome.

### RET-CLM-003

**Claim:** Higher-dose phase 2 T2D groups achieved HbA1c reductions of about 2 percentage points at week 24.

**Allowed wording:** Keep the phase 2 and population context.

**Disallowed / caution:** Do not generalise to every person or treatment combination.

### RET-CLM-004

**Claim:** TRIUMPH-1 sponsor topline reported 28.3% mean weight reduction at week 80 in the 12 mg group.

**Allowed wording:** Always identify as sponsor topline until full publication is captured.

**Disallowed / caution:** Do not call peer-reviewed primary publication evidence.

### RET-CLM-005

**Claim:** A phase 3 type 2 diabetes Lancet article record has been identified.

**Allowed wording:** State that direct extraction remains incomplete.

**Disallowed / caution:** Do not say the full article has been extracted.

### RET-CLM-006

**Claim:** Gastrointestinal adverse events are an established tolerability domain.

**Allowed wording:** Preserve dose, titration and trial context.

**Disallowed / caution:** Do not reduce safety to a binary safe/unsafe label.

### RET-CLM-007

**Claim:** A dose-dependent heart-rate increase was reported in the phase 2 obesity trial.

**Allowed wording:** Keep the phase 2 study context.

**Disallowed / caution:** Do not infer long-term clinical significance beyond the evidence.

### RET-CLM-008

**Claim:** Dysesthesia is an emerging late-stage safety signal.

**Allowed wording:** Use emerging/provisional wording.

**Disallowed / caution:** Do not present incidence as fully primary-verified while source gaps remain.

### RET-CLM-009

**Claim:** Retatrutide is Platinum Certified by Certiaura.

**Allowed wording:** State: Consolidated — Not Platinum Certified.

**Disallowed / caution:** Do not assign Platinum status until blockers are resolved.

### RET-CLM-010

**Claim:** Retatrutide has a verified universal market price.

**Allowed wording:** Use supplier- and date-specific pricing only when captured.

**Disallowed / caution:** Do not invent a universal market price.
