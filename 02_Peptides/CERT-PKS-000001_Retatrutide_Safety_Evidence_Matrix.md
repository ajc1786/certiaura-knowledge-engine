## Interpretation rule

Safety must remain multidimensional.

For each signal preserve:

- Dose
- Titration
- Population
- Duration
- Severity
- Discontinuation impact
- Source tier
- Publication status

Do not convert the matrix into a binary “safe / unsafe” label.
