## Safety interpretation rule

Do not collapse all adverse events into a single “safe / unsafe” label.

Safety assessment should preserve:

- Dose
- Titration schedule
- Population
- Duration
- Event severity
- Discontinuation
- Source tier
- Whether the result is peer reviewed
