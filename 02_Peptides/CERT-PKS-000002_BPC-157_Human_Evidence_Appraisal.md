## What cannot currently be said

The current Certiaura evidence base does **not** establish that BPC-157 is:

- clinically proven for human tendon healing;
- clinically proven for knee pain;
- clinically proven for interstitial cystitis;
- clinically proven safe for long-term human use;
- supported by a validated universal dosing regimen.

## Human evidence standard required for upgrade

For each publication, directly extract:

1. Study design
2. Prospective versus retrospective status
3. Sample size
4. Inclusion / exclusion criteria
5. Comparator or control
6. Randomisation
7. Blinding
8. Intervention formulation
9. Dose and route
10. Outcome definitions
11. Follow-up
12. Missing data
13. Adverse events
14. Statistical methods
15. Funding and conflicts
16. Risk of bias

Until those fields are directly appraised, the publications remain evidence leads rather than confirmatory clinical evidence.
