## 1. Plain-English summary

BPC-157 is a synthetic 15-amino-acid peptide with a substantial **preclinical** literature investigating tissue repair, tendon and ligament healing, vascular signalling, gastrointestinal protection and other biological effects.

The major limitation is equally important:

**Human evidence is sparse and not confirmatory.**

Certiaura therefore does not treat BPC-157 as an established human regenerative therapy.

## 6. Human dosing status

**Validated universal human dosing protocol:** No

Animal PK / ADME evidence must not be converted into a validated human dosing schedule.

Sparse real-world or pilot reports do not establish a universal dose, route or treatment cycle.
