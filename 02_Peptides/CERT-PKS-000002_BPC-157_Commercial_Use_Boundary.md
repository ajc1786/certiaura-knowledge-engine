# BPC-157 COMMERCIAL USE BOUNDARY

**Review date:** 2026-07-14

## Knowledge platform

Permitted:

- evidence summaries;
- source-status reporting;
- uncertainty reporting;
- educational comparison;
- anti-doping warnings;
- regulatory-status reporting.

## Product marketplace

Do not imply that listing a product means:

- regulatory approval;
- clinical efficacy;
- product identity;
- sterility;
- purity;
- lawful human use.

## Product Passport™

A future Product Passport must distinguish:

- identity testing;
- purity testing;
- sterility / endotoxin testing where relevant;
- supplier documentation;
- regulatory status;
- intended-use status;
- batch-level evidence.

A certificate of analysis alone does not establish clinical efficacy or regulatory approval.

## Direct sale

### United States

Do not launch on the assumption that the 2026 review has already produced final broad compounding permission.

### United Kingdom

Do not launch a human-use therapeutic route without dedicated legal / MHRA advice.

### Competitive athletes

Prominent WADA warning required.

## Commercial rule

Evidence access and product access are separate layers.

Commercial demand must not upgrade scientific or regulatory status.
