# CERT-SKS-000081 — Crystagen Identity, Organ-Claim and Human-Safety Uncertainty

**Status:** Active safety control  
**Last Review:** 2026-07-16

## Safety domain

Unknown or insufficiently closed chemical identity, unvalidated organ targeting, limited independent human evidence and unverified supplier quality.

## Prohibited assumption

The commercial product name must not be interpreted as proof that the product selectively reaches, repairs, rejuvenates or treats the named organ.

## Catalogue boundary

Availability and price do not establish identity, purity, sterility, approval or safety.
