# CERT-SKS-000036 — Oxytocin Uterine Hyperstimulation, Fetal, Fluid and Monitored-Care Risk

**Status:** Active safety control  
**Last Review:** 2026-07-16

## Safety domain

Uterine tachysystole, fetal compromise, rupture risk, hyponatraemia and cardiovascular events require monitored obstetric care.
