# CERT-SKS-000007 — BPC-157 LONG-TERM ANGIOGENIC RISK UNCERTAINTY

**Version:** 1.1.0  
**Status:** Mechanistic uncertainty  
**Last Review:** 2026-07-15

## Current evidence position

Experimental angiogenic and vascular-signalling evidence exists.

## Correct interpretation

This creates a legitimate long-term safety question.

It does not establish that BPC-157 causes cancer.

## Required future evidence

- adequate long-term toxicology;
- human exposure data;
- human adverse-event surveillance;
- evaluation in relevant high-risk populations;
- product-specific quality data.
