# CERT-SKS-000106 — Elamipretide Authorised-Product Equivalence, Hypersensitivity and Formulation Risk

**Status:** Active safety control  
**Last Review:** 2026-07-16

## Safety domain

Supplier powder cannot inherit Forzinity approval, sterile formulation, preservative, strength, label, renal controls or safety evidence. Product-specific hypersensitivity and local-reaction risks remain prominent.

## Catalogue boundary

A catalogue name and vial strength do not establish authorised-product identity, purity, sterility or safety.
