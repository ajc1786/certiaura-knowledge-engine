# CERT-SKS-000096 — Glutathione Redox, Endotoxin, Sterility and Unsupported Cosmetic-Claim Risk

**Status:** Active safety control  
**Last Review:** 2026-07-16

## Safety domain

Reduced/oxidised identity, sterile-grade suitability, endotoxin reactions and unvalidated skin-lightening claims.

## Marketplace boundary

Catalogue availability and vial strength do not establish identity, grade, route, sterility, approval or safety.
