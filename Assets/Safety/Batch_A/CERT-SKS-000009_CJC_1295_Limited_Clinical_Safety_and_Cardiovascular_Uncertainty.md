# CERT-SKS-000009 — CJC-1295 Limited Clinical Safety and Cardiovascular Uncertainty

**Status:** Active safety control  
**Last Review:** 2026-07-16

## Safety domain

Control for limited clinical data, prolonged GH/IGF-1 stimulation, cardiovascular or vasodilatory events and long-term uncertainty.

## Translation boundary

Early pharmacology does not establish long-term safety or a validated wellness protocol.
