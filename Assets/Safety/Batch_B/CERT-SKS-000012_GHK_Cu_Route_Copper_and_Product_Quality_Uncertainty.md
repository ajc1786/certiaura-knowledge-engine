# CERT-SKS-000012 — GHK-Cu Route, Copper and Product-Quality Uncertainty

**Version:** 1.1.0  
**Status:** Active safety control  
**Last Review:** 2026-07-16

## Updated evidence position

Topical and preclinical GHK-Cu evidence does not establish injectable sterility, systemic exposure, copper safety or clinical efficacy.

## Control

No universal human dosing or safety reassurance may be generated from the current evidence base.
