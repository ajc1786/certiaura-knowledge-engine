# CERT-SKS-000001 — Gastrointestinal Adverse Events

**Version:** 1.0.0  
**Status:** Primary evidence supported  
**Knowledge System:** SKS  
**Asset Type:** Safety Knowledge Object  
**Last Review:** 2026-07-14

## Summary

Nausea, diarrhoea, vomiting and other gastrointestinal adverse events recur across the Retatrutide clinical programme. Frequency and burden remain dose-, escalation- and study-specific.
