# CERT-SKS-000085 — Bremelanotide Cardiovascular, Nausea, Hyperpigmentation and Indication Safety

**Status:** Active safety control  
**Last Review:** 2026-07-16

## Safety domain

Transient blood-pressure increase, heart-rate reduction, nausea, focal hyperpigmentation, pregnancy context and restriction to the authorised clinical population.

## Product boundary

An authorised implant or autoinjector does not validate a supplier vial with a similar active-ingredient name.
