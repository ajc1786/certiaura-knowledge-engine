# CERT-SKS-000084 — Afamelanotide Skin, Pigmentation, Implant and Product-Equivalence Safety

**Status:** Active safety control  
**Last Review:** 2026-07-16

## Safety domain

Dermatological surveillance, pigmentary changes, implant-specific administration and prohibition on transferring Scenesse safety or approval to catalogue powder.

## Product boundary

An authorised implant or autoinjector does not validate a supplier vial with a similar active-ingredient name.
