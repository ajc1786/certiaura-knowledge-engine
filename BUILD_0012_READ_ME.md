# CERTIAURA BUILD 0012 — UNIVERSAL REGULATORY & STATUS INTELLIGENCE FRAMEWORK

**Build:** 0012  
**Version:** 1.0.0  
**Date:** 2026-07-15

## Purpose
Make regulatory and status intelligence mandatory for every current and future CertiAura peptide and compound.

## Locked requirement
Every applicable asset must maintain:
- dated, jurisdiction-specific regulatory status;
- competent authority;
- approval / authorisation status;
- prescribing and compounding status where relevant;
- anti-doping status where relevant;
- warnings, recalls and enforcement actions;
- source provenance;
- commercial implications;
- next review trigger;
- monitoring state;
- material-change monitoring where appropriate.
