# CERT-BKS-000144 — Short-Peptide Gene-Expression Research

**Status:** Foundation asset  
**Last Review:** 2026-07-16

## Summary

Experimental transcriptional and protein-expression effects reported for short peptides without assuming organ specificity or human efficacy.

## Control

A product name or claimed target organ is not evidence of receptor selectivity, tissue distribution or clinical efficacy.
