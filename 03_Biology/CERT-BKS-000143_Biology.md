# CERT-BKS-000143 — Tissue-Specific Peptide Claims and Evidence Translation

**Status:** Foundation asset  
**Last Review:** 2026-07-16

## Summary

Controls governing the translation of tissue-culture or organ-extract findings to a defined synthetic peptide.

## Control

A product name or claimed target organ is not evidence of receptor selectivity, tissue distribution or clinical efficacy.
