# Retatrutide Clinician Handoff Baseline

## Purpose

Defines a deterministic, source-traceable handoff report that summarises a pseudonymised journey for clinician discussion.

## Required sections

- Journey identity and reporting window.
- Latest recorded measurements and changes over time.
- Symptoms, tolerability observations and urgent indicators.
- Completed, due and overdue review items.
- Evidence and source provenance.
- Questions requiring clinician judgement.
- Safety boundary and urgent-care statement.

## Prohibited output

The handoff must not diagnose, prescribe, recommend a personalised dose, select treatment, or replace urgent clinical assessment.
