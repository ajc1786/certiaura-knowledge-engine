**Universal Asset Identifier:** CERT-RKS-000006

## Purpose

Render evidence-linked longitudinal analytics as deterministic local SVG trend panels and a machine-readable rendering manifest.

## Controls

- No external network resources.
- No scripts or persistent browser storage.
- Values, states and provenance originate from the analytics record.
- Insufficient-data and urgent-routing states remain visible.
- The visualisation is descriptive and not clinical advice.
