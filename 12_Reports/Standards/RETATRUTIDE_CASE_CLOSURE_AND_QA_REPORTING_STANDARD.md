# Retatrutide Case Closure and Quality Assurance Reporting Standard

Build provenance: `CERT-BUILD-0050`

A closure report must show the reconciled outcome state, unresolved actions, urgent-routing status, current approved export chain, evidence snapshot, quality-assurance decision, reviewer role separation and limitations.

Permitted quality-assurance decisions are `PASS`, `PASS_WITH_ACTIONS`, `REWORK_REQUIRED` and `LOCKED_URGENT_ROUTING`. A report remains draft until a human quality-assurance reviewer records a decision. The generator and quality-assurance reviewer must be different actors.
