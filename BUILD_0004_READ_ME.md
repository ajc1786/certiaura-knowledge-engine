## Purpose

Expand the Retatrutide evidence layer while preserving strict source-status distinctions.

## Evidence-status tiers used

### Tier A — Peer-reviewed primary evidence
A journal publication has been identified and can be treated as a primary scientific source.

### Tier B — Primary sponsor / official topline evidence
Results originate from the sponsor or an official release, but have not yet been replaced by a peer-reviewed primary publication.

### Tier C — Evidence lead pending primary-source capture
A credible source reports the result, but the underlying primary source has not yet been captured in the repository.
