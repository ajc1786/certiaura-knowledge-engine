# Decision Record — CERT-BUILD-0035J

**Decision:** Approve an integrated work package for Supplier portfolio risk, sourcing resilience, performance analytics and commercial controls.  
**Status:** GREEN / repository-ready  
**Date:** 2026-07-19

## Context

Build 0035I established organisation-level supplier qualification and continuous assurance. A portfolio layer was required to control concentration, critical-source dependency, comparative performance, sourcing choices and commercial decisions across multiple qualified suppliers.

## Decision

Adopt one integrated build covering portfolio concentration, approved panels, alternative-source readiness, service analytics, total-cost modelling, multi-criteria sourcing decisions, sole-source exceptions, Marketplace commercial eligibility, alerts, dashboards and Project Genesis validation.

## Controls retained

- Qualification does not verify products, batches or claims.
- Analytics and rankings are recommendation-only.
- Lowest price cannot override assurance, evidence, quality, safety or resilience blockers.
- No automatic award or sole-source approval.
- Four-eyes human approval for positive commercial decisions.
- Separate supplier qualification, Product Passport and Marketplace decisions.
- Immutable history and transparent assumptions.

## Consequence

Implementation extends the existing PPS, MPS, CIS, EKS and SYS structures. It must not create duplicate governance, registers, dashboards or a new supplier knowledge system.
