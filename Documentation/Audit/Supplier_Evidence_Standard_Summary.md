## Mandatory governance outcome

- Every product asset has one primary submission class.
- Every asset has the parenteral-risk/sterile-claim overlay.
- Authorised-product, investigational and anti-doping overlays are applied where relevant.
- All ten critical unresolved assets retain identity/composition quarantine.
- Supplier certificates do not automatically close Product Passport fields.
- No automated evidence intake may change canonical facts.
- Human scientific, quality and regulatory review is mandatory.
- Direct-sale gates remain blocked.
- No Platinum certification is assigned.

## Submission sequence

1. Submission cover sheet and checksum manifest.
2. Administrative completeness screen.
3. Manufacturer and laboratory authentication.
4. Class-specific technical review.
5. Overlay review.
6. Data-integrity review.
7. Deficiency or quarantine decision.
8. Human Product Passport field decision.
9. Change-control and expiry monitoring.
