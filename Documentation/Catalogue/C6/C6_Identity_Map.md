# C6 MELANOCORTIN IDENTITY & EVIDENCE-TRANSFER MAP

| Catalogue item | Canonical CertiAura asset | Critical boundary |
|---|---|---|
| MT-1 | CERT-PKS-000079 — Afamelanotide / Melanotan I | Supplier powder is not Scenesse implant |
| MT-2 | CERT-PKS-000009 — Melanotan II | No afamelanotide or bremelanotide approval transfer |
| PT-141 | CERT-PKS-000080 — Bremelanotide / PT-141 | Supplier powder is not Vyleesi autoinjector |

## Identity differences

- Afamelanotide is a linear alpha-MSH analogue.
- Melanotan II is a distinct cyclic melanocortin analogue.
- Bremelanotide is a distinct cyclic melanocortin analogue developed from the melanotan programme.

## Clinical-status differences

- Scenesse approval is tied to afamelanotide implant use in EPP.
- Vyleesi approval is tied to bremelanotide injection in a defined HSDD population.
- Melanotan II does not inherit either approval.

## Commercial rule

Similarity of active-ingredient naming does not establish authorised-product equivalence.
