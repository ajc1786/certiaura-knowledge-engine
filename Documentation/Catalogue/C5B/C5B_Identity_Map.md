# C5B IDENTITY & EVIDENCE-TRANSFER MAP

## Product families

- Bronchogen
- Cardiogen
- Cortagen
- Livagen
- Pancragen
- Prostamax
- Cartalax
- Chonluten
- Crystagen
- Ovagen
- Vesugen

## Current identity state

All eleven remain **supplier product-family assets** until the following are closed:

1. exact amino-acid sequence;
2. stereochemistry;
3. N- and C-terminal chemistry;
4. salt or counter-ion;
5. molecular formula and mass;
6. primary publication identity;
7. analytical reference-standard match;
8. manufacturer and batch identity.

## Evidence transfer prohibited

Evidence from:

- tissue extracts;
- another Khavinson peptide;
- a similarly named supplement;
- an animal or cell study using a different sequence;
- a patent with an unconfirmed construct;

must not be assigned to the catalogue product.

## Organ-targeting rule

Names suggesting lung, heart, brain, liver, pancreas, prostate, cartilage, ovary or vascular targeting are retained only as **supplier positioning**, not validated tissue selectivity.
