|---|---|---|
| Long R3 IGF-1 | Engineered IGF-1 analogue | Native IGF-1, mecasermin, IGF-1 DES, insulin |
| MGF | Identity-sensitive IGF-1Ec / E-domain peptide family | Full-length IGF-1, Long R3 IGF-1 |

- complete amino-acid sequence;
- sequence modifications;
- PEG molecular weight;
- PEG attachment site;
- conjugation chemistry;
- free peptide and free PEG content;
- aggregation;
- biological potency assay.
