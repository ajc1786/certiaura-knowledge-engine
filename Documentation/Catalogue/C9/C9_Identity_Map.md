## Elamipretide boundary

The canonical peptide is `D-Arg-2,6-dimethyl-Tyr-Lys-Phe-NH2`.

The United States authorised formulation is a specific elamipretide hydrochloride sterile solution. The supplier powder does not inherit:

- hydrochloride identity;
- formulation;
- preservative;
- strength;
- manufacturing;
- accelerated approval;
- clinical label;
- safety profile.

- semaglutide;
- liraglutide;
