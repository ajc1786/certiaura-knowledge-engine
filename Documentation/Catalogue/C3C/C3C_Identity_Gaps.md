# C3C IDENTITY & CRITICAL GAP MAP

## Catalogue products requiring family-level rather than molecule-level treatment

### HCG

Minimum Product Passport™ fields:

- urinary or recombinant source;
- exact molecule and brand;
- potency standard;
- excipients;
- manufacturer;
- approved indication;
- batch-specific quality certificate.

### HMG / Menotropins

Minimum fields:

- urinary source;
- FSH activity;
- LH activity or HCG contribution;
- purification process;
- composition;
- manufacturer;
- indication.

### Insulin

The current listing is clinically unusable as an identity record.

Required before any product comparison:

- exact insulin molecule or analogue;
- concentration, such as units per mL;
- total units;
- onset and duration class;
- formulation;
- vial, cartridge or pen;
- manufacturer;
- storage;
- batch and expiry.

### EPO

Required:

- epoetin or related ESA molecule;
- glycosylation/product type;
- potency;
- manufacturer;
- authorised indication;
- formulation;
- cold-chain requirements.

## Critical rule

No calculator, dosing module, administration guide or direct-sale workflow may activate while these identity gaps remain open.
