# CERTIAURA CATALOGUE SCOPE & METHOD

**Build:** 0020  
**Source:** `Peptides.pdf`  
**Review date:** 2026-07-16

## Included

Pages **1-5**, headed **Peptide Powder**.

The catalogue contains a mixture of:

- peptides;
- peptide drugs;
- proteins and glycoprotein hormones;
- biologics and peptide mixtures;
- non-peptide compounds;
- supplier blends;
- cosmetic products;
- diluents and ancillary products.

## Excluded

Pages **6-13**, which move into steroid tablets, steroid finished products and related non-peptide sections.

## Processing method

1. Capture every SKU from pages 1-5.
2. Consolidate strengths under preliminary product families.
3. Cross-reference existing CertiAura assets.
4. Flag aliases, likely mislabelling and unresolved identities.
5. Separate blends from individual peptide assets.
6. Separate non-peptide and ancillary items from PKS.
7. Allocate future UAIs only after identity review.
8. Review the catalogue in smaller scientific batches.

## Catalogue warning

The supplier catalogue is evidence of what is offered and at what listed price.

It is **not** evidence of:

- purity;
- sterility;
- sequence identity;
- potency;
- approval;
- lawful human use;
- equivalence to material used in published research.
