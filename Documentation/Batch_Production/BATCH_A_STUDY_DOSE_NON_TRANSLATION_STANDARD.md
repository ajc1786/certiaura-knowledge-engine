# BATCH A STUDY-DOSE NON-TRANSLATION STANDARD

## Rule

Study dose, route and frequency fields are retained for evidence integrity.

They must not be presented as:

- personal dosing advice;
- a reconstitution protocol;
- a recommended cycle;
- proof of equivalent exposure across products;
- a human conversion from animal dosing.

## Additional controls

- CJC-1295 evidence applies to the DAC compound studied.
- Ipamorelin postoperative dosing was an inpatient intravenous study regimen.
- MOTS-c injection regimens were primarily animal experiments.
- Tesamorelin evidence is indication- and product-context dependent.
