## Review standard for Builds 0021-0028

Each batch will include:

- canonical identity and aliases;
- peptide / protein / compound classification;
- evidence maturity;
- high-value primary-source map;
- safety controls;
- monitoring requirements;
- FDA, MHRA, EMA and WADA status where applicable;
- commercial boundary;
- catalogue price and strength register;
- Product Passport™ gaps;
- UAI allocation only after identity resolution.
