# BATCH B IDENTITY-EVIDENCE TRANSFER GATE

## GHK-Cu

Do not transfer evidence between:

- free GHK;
- GHK-Cu;
- topical copper tripeptide-1 products;
- injectable GHK-Cu;
- multi-peptide blends.

## Thymosin Beta-4 / TB-500

Do not transfer full-length thymosin beta-4 clinical evidence to:

- Ac-LKKTETQ / TB-500;
- unverified fragments;
- supplier products without sequence confirmation.

## Melanocortins

Do not transfer evidence between:

- Melanotan II;
- afamelanotide;
- bremelanotide;
- unverified nasal tanning products.

## Epitalon

Do not transfer evidence between:

- synthetic AEDG Epitalon;
- epithalamin pineal extract;
- other pineal peptide blends.
