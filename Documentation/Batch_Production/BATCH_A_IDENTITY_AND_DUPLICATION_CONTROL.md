# PEPTIDE BATCH A — IDENTITY & DUPLICATION CONTROL

## CJC-1295 warning

`CJC-1295 with DAC` and `modified GRF (1-29)` are not the same canonical object.

Marketplace naming often uses:

- CJC-1295
- CJC-1295 with DAC
- CJC-1295 no DAC
- modified GRF (1-29)

CertiAura must not merge supplier records unless the chemical identity is verified.

## Tesamorelin

Approved product identity must remain separate from unverified research-grade supplier material.

## Ipamorelin

The INN / development identity must be separated from blends and stacks.

## MOTS-c

Native MOTS-c identity must be separated from modified analogues, blends and products with unverified sequence or purity.
