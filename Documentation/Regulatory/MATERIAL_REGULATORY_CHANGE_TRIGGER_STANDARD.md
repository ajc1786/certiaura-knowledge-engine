# MATERIAL REGULATORY CHANGE TRIGGER STANDARD

A material change is any development that could alter user safety, legal status, approval, prescribing, compounding, direct sale, marketplace listing, athlete eligibility or public claims.

## Mandatory response
Capture source → verify date → compare previous status → update record → review claims → review commercial boundary → update Product Passport™ if relevant → log change.

Do not notify for duplicated reporting, unchanged schedules or speculation without status impact.
