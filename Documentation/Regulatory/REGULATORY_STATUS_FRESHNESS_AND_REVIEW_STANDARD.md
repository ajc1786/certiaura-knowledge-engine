# REGULATORY STATUS FRESHNESS & REVIEW STANDARD

Review immediately after a material event such as:
- approval or rejection;
- regulator meeting recommendation;
- warning or recall;
- enforcement action;
- compounding-status change;
- annual anti-doping list update.

A record becomes STALE when its review date passes, a known material event occurs without update, or the cited authority supersedes the source.

A record without a recent check is not automatically current.
