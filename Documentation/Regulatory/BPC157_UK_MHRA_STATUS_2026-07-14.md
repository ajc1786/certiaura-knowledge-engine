## Core control

Certiaura must not state:

- “MHRA approved”;
- “licensed medicine in the UK”;
- “legal for human sale because it says research use only”;

unless that statement is supported by current authoritative legal and regulatory advice.

## Why “research use only” is not treated as a complete legal solution

The commercial and regulatory analysis depends on:

- intended use;
- presentation;
- claims;
- supply chain;
- product status;
- route to market.

A label alone should not be treated as overriding those factors.
