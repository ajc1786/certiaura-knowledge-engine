## Approval status

**BPC-157 is not FDA-approved.**

## Compounding status

The regulatory position is **active and transitional**.

As of 2026-07-14:

- FDA review of BPC-157 for possible use as a bulk substance in certain compounded preparations is ongoing.

- FDA staff review released before the meeting reportedly highlighted limited evidence of safety and benefit.

## Critical legal / commercial boundary

Certiaura must not state that:

- BPC-157 is FDA-approved;
- BPC-157 has completed a formal drug-approval pathway;
- broad lawful compounding permission is finally established;
- an advisory-committee meeting itself equals approval.
