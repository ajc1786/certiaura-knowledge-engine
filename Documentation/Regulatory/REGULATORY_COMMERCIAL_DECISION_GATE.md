# REGULATORY COMMERCIAL DECISION GATE

Before any knowledge asset progresses to product activity, confirm:
- jurisdiction;
- regulatory classification;
- approval status;
- supply legality;
- prescribing / dispensing status;
- compounding status;
- import / manufacture requirements;
- advertising restrictions;
- anti-doping implications;
- product-quality requirements;
- Product Passport™ readiness;
- need for legal review.

Gate outputs:
GO · GO WITH CONDITIONS · HOLD · LEGAL REVIEW REQUIRED · REGULATORY REVIEW REQUIRED · NOT PERMITTED
