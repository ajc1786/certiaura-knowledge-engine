# CERT-PKS-000001 — Retatrutide DEFINED-SCOPE SOURCE PACKET

**Build:** 0035C  
**Status date:** 2026-07-17  
**Scope state:** COMPLETE WITHIN DEFINED SCOPE  
**Exhaustive systematic review:** NO

## Product or regulatory boundary

Retatrutide remains an investigational GIP, GLP-1 and glucagon receptor agonist. TRIUMPH-1 completed in April 2026 without posted registry results, while additional phase 3 studies remain active. A 2026 phase 3 type 2 diabetes trial has been published, but no catalogue RT vial inherits sponsor clinical-material identity or approval.

## Human evidence boundary

The 2023 phase 2 obesity trial reported dose-related weight reduction over 48 weeks. TRANSCEND-T2D-1 reported phase 3 glycaemic and weight outcomes in 2026. Current phase 3 TRIUMPH studies examine obesity and related complications, but trial material and protocols remain sponsor controlled.

## Safety boundary

Published retatrutide studies report predominantly gastrointestinal adverse events, with dose-related heart-rate increases in the phase 2 obesity trial. Longer-term cardiovascular, gallbladder, pancreatic, retinal, reproductive and rare-event risks remain subject to ongoing phase 3 and post-development evaluation.
