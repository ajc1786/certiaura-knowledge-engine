# CERT-PKS-000015 — Survodutide DEFINED-SCOPE SOURCE PACKET

**Build:** 0035C  
**Status date:** 2026-07-17  
**Scope state:** COMPLETE WITHIN DEFINED SCOPE  
**Exhaustive systematic review:** NO

## Product or regulatory boundary

Survodutide remains an investigational glucagon/GLP-1 receptor dual agonist. SYNCHRONIZE-1 completed in February 2026 and phase 3 obesity results were published in June 2026. The cardiovascular-outcome study reached primary completion in May 2026, but no catalogue SUR10 vial inherits sponsor trial-material identity or approval.

## Human evidence boundary

Phase 3 SYNCHRONIZE-1 evaluated survodutide in adults with obesity without diabetes. A phase 2 biopsy-based MASH trial reported improvement in MASH without worsening fibrosis. Other phase 3 studies cover diabetes, cardiovascular safety, China and metabolic liver disease.

## Safety boundary

The published programme reports frequent gastrointestinal adverse events, especially nausea, diarrhoea and vomiting. Long-term cardiovascular, hepatic, gallbladder, pancreatic, nutritional and rare-event risks remain under active phase 3 evaluation. Survodutide is not represented as an approved medicine in this source packet.
