# CERT-PKS-000013 — Cagrilintide DEFINED-SCOPE SOURCE PACKET

**Build:** 0035C  
**Status date:** 2026-07-17  
**Scope state:** COMPLETE WITHIN DEFINED SCOPE  
**Exhaustive systematic review:** NO

## Product or regulatory boundary

Cagrilintide remains an investigational long-acting amylin analogue. REDEFINE 1 included a cagrilintide monotherapy arm and continues an extension through 2026. The United States regulatory application filed in December 2025 concerns the fixed combination CagriSema, not catalogue CGL vials or cagrilintide monotherapy.

## Human evidence boundary

A 706-participant phase 2 dose-finding trial established cagrilintide monotherapy weight-management evidence. REDEFINE 1 subsequently included cagrilintide 2.4 mg as an active monotherapy arm alongside CagriSema, semaglutide and placebo. Separate monotherapy phase 3 RENEW studies were initiated.

## Safety boundary

The dominant reported adverse events are gastrointestinal disorders and administration-site reactions. Amylin-related gastric, nutritional and interaction effects remain formulation- and dose-specific. CagriSema safety and submission status cannot be transferred to unverified cagrilintide powder.
