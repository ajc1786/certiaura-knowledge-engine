# CERT-PKS-000014 — Mazdutide DEFINED-SCOPE SOURCE PACKET

**Build:** 0035C

**Scope state:** COMPLETE WITHIN DEFINED SCOPE  
**Exhaustive systematic review:** NO

## Product or regulatory boundary

China's NMPA approved an exact mazdutide finished product for chronic weight management in June 2025 and for glycaemic control in adults with type 2 diabetes in September 2025. The approvals are product-, manufacturer-, dose-, formulation- and jurisdiction-specific. Catalogue MDT5 and MDT10 powders are not that authorised product.

## Human evidence boundary

GLORY-1 established phase 3 weight-management evidence for 4 mg and 6 mg mazdutide in Chinese adults. DREAMS-1 established phase 3 monotherapy evidence in type 2 diabetes. GLORY-2 reported 9 mg evidence in 2026, while the 9 mg supplementary application remained under Chinese regulatory review.

## Safety boundary

The phase 3 programme reports predominantly gastrointestinal adverse events, including nausea, vomiting and diarrhoea. Product-specific titration, hepatic, gallbladder, pancreatic, cardiovascular and rare-event controls require the exact Chinese label and ongoing evidence. No non-Chinese authorisation is inferred.
