# CERT-PKS-000016 — Liraglutide DEFINED-SCOPE SOURCE PACKET

**Build:** 0035B  
**Status date:** 2026-07-16  
**Scope state:** COMPLETE WITHIN DEFINED SCOPE

## Authorised-product boundary

Liraglutide is used in exact authorised SAXENDA and VICTOZA products and now has additional authorised-generic and European hybrid-product contexts. The current SAXENDA label is a 6 mg/mL prefilled-pen solution for defined weight-management populations. Catalogue LL powders are not an authorised finished product.

## Human evidence boundary

The SCALE obesity trial established weight-management evidence for sponsor-controlled liraglutide 3.0 mg. LEADER established cardiovascular-outcome evidence for a defined liraglutide diabetes product and population. Evidence must remain product, dose, route and indication specific.

## Safety boundary

Current SAXENDA controls include thyroid C-cell tumour warning, pancreatitis, gallbladder disease, hypoglycaemia in relevant settings, heart-rate increase, acute kidney injury from volume depletion, severe gastrointestinal reactions, hypersensitivity, pregnancy risk and pulmonary aspiration during anaesthesia or deep sedation.
