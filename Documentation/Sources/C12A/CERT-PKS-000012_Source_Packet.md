## Authorised-product boundary

Tirzepatide is the active substance in exact authorised MOUNJARO and ZEPBOUND products. The current ZEPBOUND label covers chronic weight management and moderate-to-severe obstructive sleep apnoea in adults with obesity. Catalogue TR powders do not inherit any authorised product's formulation, pen system, concentration, quality or indication.

## Human evidence boundary

SURMOUNT-1 was a phase 3 randomised trial in 2,539 adults with obesity or overweight and a weight-related complication without diabetes. Product-specific trials and labels also support other exact indications. None validates catalogue lyophilised vials.

## Safety boundary

Current label controls include thyroid C-cell tumour warning, severe gastrointestinal reactions, acute kidney injury from volume depletion, gallbladder disease, pancreatitis, hypersensitivity, hypoglycaemia with relevant concomitant medicines, diabetic-retinopathy complications, pregnancy risk and pulmonary aspiration during anaesthesia or deep sedation.
