# MASTER SUPPLIER QUESTIONNAIRE

## A. Legal entity and supply chain

1. Legal manufacturer name, registration number and address.
2. Manufacturing-site address for the submitted product.
3. Relationship of respondent: manufacturer, authorised agent, distributor or broker.
4. Complete chain from manufacturer to CertiAura/customer.
5. Current manufacturing, wholesale, import or distribution authorisations.
6. Regulatory inspections, warning letters, suspensions, recalls or enforcement in the last five years.

## B. Quality system

1. Quality-unit structure and named responsible person.
2. Current GMP, ISO or equivalent certificates and full scope.
3. Supplier qualification and change-control procedures.
4. Deviation, out-of-specification, complaint and recall procedures.
5. Data-integrity governance and electronic-system controls.
6. Record-retention period.

## C. Product identity

1. Proper name, sequence/structure/composition and trade names.
2. Salt, termini, stereochemistry, conjugation, glycosylation or mixture definition.
3. Manufacturer product code and batch number.
4. Reference standard and traceability.
5. Known identity conflicts or alternative names.

## D. Manufacturing

1. Synthesis, expression, extraction, blending or purification summary.
2. Starting materials and critical suppliers.
3. Critical process parameters and in-process controls.
4. Lyophilisation, sterilisation or aseptic-process summary.
5. Rework, reprocessing or pooling practices.
6. Batch size and scale.

## E. Analytical control

1. Specifications and methods.
2. Method validation or verification.
3. Raw chromatograms, spectra, audit trails and calculations.
4. Impurity identification and reporting thresholds.
5. Peptide/protein content or potency independent of area-percent purity.
6. Stability-indicating capability.

## F. Sterile quality

1. Sterile or non-sterile status.
2. Sterilisation/aseptic process and validation.
3. Sterility, endotoxin and particulate testing.
4. Container-closure integrity.
5. Environmental monitoring and media fills.
6. In-use and transport controls.

## G. Stability and logistics

1. Stability protocol, conditions and batches.
2. Proposed shelf life or retest period.
3. Temperature excursions and shipping validation.
4. Light, oxygen and moisture sensitivity.
5. After-opening or post-preparation stability where applicable.

## H. Regulatory and comparator status

1. Every jurisdiction in which the exact product is authorised, registered or supplied.
2. Exact authorised or trial comparator claimed.
3. Evidence supporting comparability.
4. Current warnings, recalls or safety restrictions.
5. Anti-doping review where applicable.

## Declaration

The authorised signatory confirms that the submission is complete, authentic and includes failed, repeated and invalidated data relevant to the submitted batch.
