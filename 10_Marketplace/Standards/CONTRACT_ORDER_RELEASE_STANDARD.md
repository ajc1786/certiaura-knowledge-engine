# Contract and Order Release Standard

1. Contracts require separate commercial and quality approval, current legal review, conflict declarations and expiry control.
2. Orders inherit no approval from price ranking, supplier panel inclusion or prior orders.
3. The order-release timestamp must be within all gate validity windows.
4. Substitution of supplier, product, batch, route or jurisdiction voids the release and requires re-review.
5. Emergency release requires a documented exception, legal basis, risk owner, expiry and retrospective review; it cannot be created by automation.
6. Automation may block or prepare a release pack only.
